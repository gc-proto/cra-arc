CVITP tax preparation procedures

The following procedures will provide you with the knowledge and tools to prepare tax returns as a CVITP volunteer.

To ensure the program's success, organizations and volunteers must safeguard taxpayer information and understand their responsibilities in this respect.

Important note

  • Do not use or disclose the individual's information for any purpose other than to complete their tax return
  • Store the individual's information securely in a locked drawer or cabinet and never leave their information unattended
  • Be mindful of your surroundings when speaking with individuals, especially in a virtual clinic setting when other people are present or there are nearby devices like Google Home or Alexa
  • Tell your community organization and your Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) coordinator immediately if a security breach---such as lost, stolen, or compromised data---occurs
