Takeaway points

Steps to follow

  1. Review the background information and the required slips
  2. Select + add dependant to add both dependent children and complete the Dependant Identification section for each child
  3. Select Child care in the left side menu of the dependant’s profile, select the type of child care expenses and enter the amount paid for that child

When claiming child care expenses in UFile, the amount paid per child must be entered in each child’s individual profile.

In this case, the amount of $1,011 is entered under Rose’s profile and $3,160 is entered under Rhys’ profile. UFile automatically adds up the total amount of eligible child care expenses and enters it on line 21400 of Candice’s tax return.
