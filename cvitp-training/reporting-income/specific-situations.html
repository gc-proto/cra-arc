Tax return for Status Indian

  •  select Status Indian from the left side menu
  •  answer Yes to the statement Please confirm that you are a registered Indian, or entitled to be registered as an Indian under the Indian Act

Instructions for T4A(P)

  •  enter the tax exempt amount from box 20 in the field Exempt CPP/QPP benefits, and;
  •  enter the T4A(P) as you normally would

Proceed to next step

Instructions for a T5007 or letter for social assistance payments received

  • enter the tax exempt amount from box 10 in the field Exempt workers’ compensation benefits
  • enter the tax exempt social assistance amount from box 11, the amount in the letter from the band council or the amount the individual tells you, in the field Exempt social assistance payments
  • enter the T5007, or the amount in the letter from the band council, or the amount the individual tells you, as usual for Social assistance payments in a T5007

Proceed to next step

Instructions for a T4A, COVID-19 benefits

  • enter the tax exempt amount from boxes 197, 200, 202, 203, 204 and 211 in the field Exempt benefits paid due to the COVID-19
  • enter the T4A as usual

Proceed to next step

Instructions for a T5 or interest income without a tax slip

  • enter the tax exempt interest in the field Exempt interest income, and;
  • enter the T5 and interest income as usual

Proceed to next step

Instructions for a T4A box 48

  • select Yes to the statement Please confirm that you are a registered Indian, or entitled to be registered as an Indian under the Indian Act
  • enter the tax exempt amount in the T2125 Self-employment income

Proceed to next step

Instructions for tips and gratuities

  • enter the tax exempt amount of tips and gratuities in the field Other employment exempt income
  • enter the total amount of tips and gratuities as you normally would

Proceed to next step

Newcomers

  • enter the Date of entry for the individual
  • if the individual identified they had a spouse, there will be a second question in this section: Your net income while you were living in Canada with your spouse
  • leave this field blank to allow UFile to use the amount from line 23600 considering both spouses share the same date of entry and have been living together (married or common-law) since that date
  • otherwise, enter the individual’s net income while living in Canada with their spouse

Proceed to next step

Newcomers

  • scroll down in the UFile screen
  • Canadian sourced income (excluding Part XIII) earned when you were not a resident of Canada:
  • select the Source of income from the drop down menu
  • enter the amount in Canadian dollars
  • if the individual did not have any income, select any source of income and enter 0.00$
  • Canadian source Part XIII income PLUS foreign sourced income earned when you were not a resident of Canada:
    • enter the Name of the country
    • select the Source of income from the drop down menu
    • enter the amount in Canadian dollars
    • if the individual did not have any income, select any source of income and enter 0.00$ in the next field
  • click Next
