Instructions for newcomers
  • select Immigrant, emigrant, non-resident from the left side menu
  • click the + sign next to You immigrated to Canada in 2022 for:
  • enter the Date of entry for the individual
  • if the individual identified they had a spouse, there will be a second question in this section: Your net income while you were living in Canada with your spouse
    • leave this field blank to allow UFile to use the amount from line 23600 considering both spouses share the same date of entry and have been living together (married or common-law) since that date
    • otherwise, enter the individual’s net income while living in Canada with their spouse
  • scroll down in the UFile screen
  • Canadian sourced income (excluding Part XIII) earned when you were not a resident of Canada:
  • select the Source of income from the drop down menu
  • enter the amount in Canadian dollars
  • if the individual did not have any income, select any source of income and enter 0.00$
  • Canadian source Part XIII income PLUS foreign sourced income earned when you were not a resident of Canada:
    • enter the Name of the country
    • select the Source of income from the drop down menu
    • enter the amount in Canadian dollars
    • if the individual did not have any income, select any source of income and enter 0.00$ in the next field
  • click Next
Instructions for a newcomer spouse
  • click the + sign next to You are a Canadian resident and your spouse immigrated to Canada in 2022 for:
  • an individual who is already a Canadian resident and their spouse or common-law partner immigrated to Canada in 2022
  • Your net income while you were living in Canada with your spouse
  • click Next
