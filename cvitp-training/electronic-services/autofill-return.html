CRA electronic services

Auto-fill my return (AFR)

Auto-fill my return (AFR) is a secure CRA service used to fill in parts of an individual’s federal income tax and benefit return automatically with information that the CRA has available at the time of filing. CVITP volunteers can use this service to complete 2017 and subsequent year returns.
