T1204 summary - Government services contract payments

On this page

  • What is a T1204 summary

What is a T1204 summary

The T1204 summary (T1204SUM) represents the total of the information reported on all of the T1204 slips you prepared for each recipient for the calendar year.

You can get a Form T1204SUM in a PDF or PDF fillable/saveable format to file on paper.

Sample

What to report

  • Report all amounts in Canadian dollars, even if they were paid in another currency. Learn more about the average exchange rates: Exchange rates
  • Fill out a separate T1204 summary for each of your payroll accounts. The totals you report on your T1204 summary have to agree with the totals of all amounts you reported on your T1204 slips for that payroll account
  • If you do not have any amounts to report on a T1204 slip or summary, there is no need to send the CRA either form
  • Do not change your address using the T1204 summary. Learn more: Make changes to your account

How to fill out

Year

Enter the last 2 digits of the calendar year for which you are filing the return.

Payer's account number

Enter your 15-character payroll account number  (for example, 123456789RP0001).

Payer's name and address

Enter the name of the federal department, agency, or Crown corporation. Below the name, enter the mailing address, including the province, territory, postal code, and country.

Box 88 – Total number of T1204 slips filed

Enter the total number of T1204 slips that you are filing with the T1204 summary.

Box 82 – Total service payments only

Add the amounts in box 82 of all T1204 slips. Report the total on line 82.

Box 84 – Total mixed services and goods payments

Add the amounts in box 84 of all T1204 slips. Report the total on line 84.

Person to contact about this return

Enter the name and telephone number of a contact person that the CRA can call to get or clarify information you reported on the T1204 summary.
