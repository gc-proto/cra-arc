T4A summary - Information for payers

On this page

What is a T4 summary

The T4A summary represents the total of the information reported on all of the T4A slips you prepared for each recipient for the calendar year.

You can get a T4ASUM in a PDF or PDF fillable/saveable to file on paper.

Sample

Get ready to fill out

  • Report all amounts in Canadian dollars, even if they were paid in another currency, learn more on the average exchange rates: Exchange rates
  • Fill out a separate T4A summary for each T4A information return submitted on one of your payroll program accounts. The totals you report on your T4A summary have to agree with the totals you report on your T4A slips
  • If you have not reported any amounts on the T4A slip or summary, there is no need to send the CRA a form
  • Do not change your address using the T4A summary, learn more: Make changes to your account

How to fill out

Year

Enter the last two digits of the calendar year for which you are filing the return.

Payer's account number

Enter your 15-character business number (for example, 123456789RP0001).

Name and address of payer

Enter your operating or trading name, and address.

Line 088 – Total number of T4A slips filed

Enter the total number of T4A slips that you are including with the T4A Summary.

Lines 016 to 048

Add the amounts in boxes 16 to 48 on all T4A slips. Enter the total on lines 016 to 048.

Line 101 – Other information

Add the amounts in box 101 on all T4A slips. Enter the total on line 101.

Line 022 – Income tax deducted

Add the amounts in box 22 on all T4A slips. Enter the total on line 22.

Line 082 – Remittances

Enter the total amount of tax you remitted to the CRA. You can get this amount from your most recent Form PD7A.

Difference

Subtract line 82 from line 22.

If the amount is negative, there is an overpayment. Enter the result on line 84.

If this amount is positive, there is a balance due. Enter the result on line 86.

If there is no difference between the total deductions you reported and the amount you remitted for the year, leave lines 84 and 86 blank.

Line 084 – Overpayment

If the amount on line 82 is more than the amount on line 22 (and you do not have to file another type of return for this payroll program account), enter the difference on line 84.

Attach or send a note giving the reason for the overpayment and whether you want the CRA to transfer this amount to another account or refund the overpayment to you.

Line 086 – Balance due

If the amount on line 22 is more than the amount on line 82, enter the difference on line 86.

Lines 071, 072, and 073 – Registration number(s) for RPP or DPSP

Enter the seven-digit registration number(s) that the CRA gave you (up to a maximum of three) for your registered pension plans or deferred profit sharing plans.

Lines 074 and 075 – Canadian-controlled private corporations or unincorporated employers

Enter the social insurance numbers of any proprietors or principal owners.

Lines 076 and 078 – Person to contact about this return

Enter the name and telephone number of a contact person that the CRA can call to get or clarify information you reported on the information return.

Certification

The authorized person must sign and date the T4A Summary.
