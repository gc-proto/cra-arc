Fill out the slips and summaries

Payroll information returns contain slips and a summary. Depending on the type of income you pay, you may have to fill out and issue more than one type of return.

Types

Select the type of information return to find out who must fill it out, what to report and when to issue slips.

  • T4 slip - Information for employers
  • T4 summary - Information for employers
  • T4A slip - Information for payers
  • T4A summary - Information for payers
  • T4A-RCA slip - Retirement Compensation Arrangement (RCA) trust
  • T4A-RCA summary - Retirement Compensation Arrangement (RCA) trust
  • T4A-NR slip - Payments to non-residents for services provided in Canada
  • T4A-NR summary - Payments to non-residents for services provided in Canada
  • T4PS slip - Statement of Employees Profit-Sharing Plan Allocations and Payments
  • T4PS summary - Statement of Employees Profit-Sharing Plan Allocations and Payments
  • T1204 slip - Government services contract payments
  • T1204 summary - Government services contract payments
  • T5018 slip - Statement of contract payments
  • T5018 summary- Statement of contract payments
