T4PS summary - Statement of Employees Profit Sharing Plan Allocations and Payments

On this page

  • What is a T4PS summary
  • What to report
  • How to fill out

What is a T4PS summary

The T4PS summary (T4PS-SUM) represents the total of the information reported on all of the T4PS slips you prepared for each employee for the calendar year.

You can get a Form T4PS in a PDF or PDF fillable/saveable format to file on paper.

Sample

What to report

What is an employees profit sharing plan (EPSP) and how to make an election to have the arrangement qualify as an EPSP
  • Report all amounts in Canadian dollars, even if they were paid in another currency. Learn more about the average exchange rates: Exchange rates
  • Fill out a separate T4PS summary for each of your payroll accounts. The totals you report on your T4PS summary have to agree with the totals of all amounts you reported on your T4PS slips for that payroll account
  • If you do not have any amounts to report on a T4PS slip or summary, there is no need to send the CRA either form
  • Do not change your address using the T4PS summary. Learn more: Make changes to your account

How to fill out

Period Summary filed for calendar year 2024 only

For T4PS summary filed for 2024, enter the ending date of the period of the calendar year in which you allocated or paid the amount to the employee, either:

  • For January 1, 2024 to June 24, 2024, enter 20240624.

  • For June 25, 2024 to December 31, 2024, enter 20241231.

If you are reporting amounts for both periods, complete a separate T4PS Summary for each period.

Year Summary filed for before and after calendar year 2024

For T4PS summary filed before and after 2024, enter the 4 digits of the calendar year in which you allocated or paid the amount to the employee.

Employer's account number

Enter your 15-character payroll account number  (for example, 123456789RP0001).

Name and address of employer

Enter your legal name, your operating or trading name (if it differs from your legal name), and address.

Name of employees profit sharing plan

Enter the name of the plan.

Name of trustee

Enter the name of the trustee of the plan.

Line 53 – Total number of T4PS slips filed

Enter the total number of T4PS slips that you are filing with the T4PS summary.

Line 24 – Actual amount of dividends other than eligible dividends

Add the amounts in box 24 of all T4PS slips. Report the total on line 24.

Line 30 – Actual amount of eligible dividends

Add the amounts in box 30 of all T4PS slips. Report the total on line 30.

Line 34 – Capital gains or losses

Add the amounts in box 34 of all T4PS slips. Report the total on line 34.

Line 35 – Other employment income

Add the amounts in box 35 of all T4PS slips. Report the total on line 35.

Line 36 – Total amount forfeited due to withdrawal from plan

Add the amounts in box 36 of all T4PS slips. Report the total on line 36.

Line 37 – Foreign non-business income

Add the amounts in box 37 of all T4PS slips. Report the total on line 37.

Line 38 – Foreign capital gains or losses

Add the amounts in box 38 of all T4PS slips. Report the total on line 38.

Line 39 – Foreign non-business income tax

Add the amounts in box 39 of all T4PS slips. Report the total on line 39.

Line 41 – Employees profit sharing plan contributions

Add the amounts in box 41 of all T4PS slips. Report the total on line 41.

Lines 76 and 78 – Person to contact about this return

Enter the name and telephone number of a contact person that the CRA can call to get or clarify information you reported on the T4PS summary.

Certification

An authorized person must sign and date the T4PS summary.
