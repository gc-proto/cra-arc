Notice of assessment (PD7D)

What is a PD7D notice

The PD7D is sent when amounts are assessed on your payroll deduction account.

The PD7D consists of both:

  • The notice of assessment
  • A remittance voucher that is used to pay an existing balance owing

It is not a statement of account.

The PD7D cannot be used to remit current payroll remittances. You must use the PD7A to remit (pay).

Learn more: Remit (pay) payroll deductions contributions

Responding to a PD7D notice

The method to respond to a PD7D depends on the reason for the assessment.

  • Assessment related to a PIER report: Option 1

    You must review the original PIER report.

    Learn more: Receiving a PIER

  • Assessment not related to a PIER report: Option 2

    You are not required to respond to the PD7D if you agree with our calculations and you will be remitting (paying) the exact amount shown on the notice of assessment.

What if you disagree with the PD7D notice of assessment

If you disagree, you may want to register a formal dispute.

Learn more: Service feedback, objections, appeals, disputes, and relief measures
