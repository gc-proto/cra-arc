Before you register

Information you need to open an account

You must have all of the following when you register:

  • date employees receive their first wages
  • months covered for payroll of employees' wages
  • type of pay period (for example, weekly, quarterly)
  • number of employees
  • payroll service name (if any)
  • country of parent or affiliate, if you have a foreign owned corporation
  • name of franchisor and country of franchise's head office (if any)

You will also need all of the following if it differs from the basic business information above:

  • mailing address
  • physical address

Learn more: Business Registration Online – What you will need.
