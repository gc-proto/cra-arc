Where a full-time remote work agreement was made, answer a few questions to help the CRA determine if your employee can reasonably be considered "attached to an establishment of the employer" under the new CRA administrative policy.

All of the facts relevant to your employee's situation will be considered to determine if your employee can be reasonably considered "attached to an establishment of the employer.

Primary indicator to consider

Q1. Did your employee physically report to one of your establishments in Canada immediately before entering the full-time remote work agreement?

Primary indicator

Under the new CRA administrative policy on remote work, the establishment is the one to which they would be reasonably considered to be attached where your employee:

  • Physically reported to one of your establishments in Canada immediately before entering the full-time remote work agreement
  • Circumstances or the nature of their duties did not changed after the full-time remote work agreement was entered into
