Remote worker province of employment (POE) questionnaire

Use the Remote work online questionnaire to send a request for the CRA to determine the province of employment.

Your feedback may be about:

  • a service complaint, suggestion, or compliment
  • an accessibility barrier or the CRA Accessibility Plan

The online questionnaire cannot accept additional supporting documents. If we need any additional information, we will ask for it when we contact you.

Older browser versions may not be supported.

After you submit the questionnaire, print or save a copy of the confirmation page for your records.
