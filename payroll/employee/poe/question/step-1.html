Request to obtain guidance to determine the province of employment for your remote employee - Step 2 of 4

You (as the employer) can send a request to the CRA to obtain guidance to determine the province of employment (POE) for your employee working entirely remotely. The response to you request will not be an official decision made by the CRA.

Where a full-time remote work agreement was made, answer a few questions to help the CRA determine if your employee working entirely remotely can reasonably be considered "attached to an establishment of the employer" under the new CRA administrative policy.

All of the facts relevant to your employee's situation will be considered to determine if your employee can be reasonably considered “attached to an establishment of the employer”.

The CRA may ask for more information later if your situation is complex.

Primary indicator

Did your employee physically report to one of your establishments in Canada immediately before entering the full-time remote work agreement?

  • Yes

    Did your employee's circumstances or the nature of their duties change after the full-time remote work agreement was entered into?

    • Yes
    • No
  • No

Payroll and corporate tax processing

From which of your establishments is your payroll department or payroll records located or where the payroll processing for your employee is done?

What is the province or territory of where you are registered for your employee's worker's compensation coverage?

If you are a corporation conducting business across multiple provinces, in which province or territory is your employee's salary expensed?

Secondary indicators

From which of your establishments would your employee attend in-person meetings, through any type of communication?

From which of your establishments does your employee receive or would your employee receive work-related material, equipment or associated instructions and assistance?

From which of your establishments does your employee come or would come to receive instructions in-person from you regarding their employment duties, through any type of communication?

Which of your establishments is responsible for or supervises the employee, as indicated in the contractual agreements between you and the employee?

From which of your establishment does your employee would report based on the nature of the duties performed by the employee?

Instructions

  1. After filling in all the required fields, click on the “Print” button at the bottom of the form.
  2. In the following pop-up window, click the dropdown menu name “Destination” or “Printer” and select the “Save as PDF” or “Print as PDF” option.
  3. Click on the “Save” button at the bottom of the window.
  4. The “Save As” window will appear, allowing you to name the file and choose the file save destination on your device.
  5. Submit your request by email to: PLS-SPL@cra-arc.gc.ca, do not include protected information.
  6. A CRA officer should respond within 30 business days from the date the request is sent. They may contact you by phone as part of the review process.
