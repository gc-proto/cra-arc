Request to obtain guidance to determine the province of employment for your employee working entirely remotely

You (as the employer) can send a request to the CRA to obtain guidance to determine if your employee working entirely remotely can reasonably be considered "attached to an establishment of the employer" under the new CRA administrative policy.

All of the facts relevant to your employee's situation will be considered to determine if your employee can be reasonably considered “attached to an establishment of the employer”. The response to you request will not be an official decision made by the CRA.

Once you filled out all required fields, you can print your request. Information is not saved on this page. If you leave this page without printing you will need to enter your information again.

The CRA may ask for more information later if your situation is complex.

Review and send request
  1. After filling in all the required fields, click on the “Print preview” button.
  2. In the following pop-up window, click the dropdown menu name “Destination” or “Printer” and select the “Save as PDF” or “Print as PDF” option.
  3. Click on the “Save” button at the bottom of the window.
  4. The “Save As” window will appear, allowing you to name the file and choose the file save destination on your device.
  5. Submit your request by email to: PLS-SPL@cra-arc.gc.ca, do not include protected information.
  6. A CRA officer should respond within 30 business days from the date the request is sent. They may contact you by phone as part of the review process.
