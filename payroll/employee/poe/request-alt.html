Before you begin

You (as the employer) can send a request to the CRA to obtain guidance to determine if your employee working entirely remotely can reasonably be considered "attached to an establishment of the employer" under the new CRA administrative policy.

If you do not meet all of the below conditions, the new CRA administrative policy on remote work does not apply to your situation. In this situation, continue to: Determine the province of employment (POE).
