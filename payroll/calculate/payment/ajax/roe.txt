<p>Once you have calculated the earnings of the worker, you also <strong>have</strong> to report on the <abbr title="record of employment">ROE</abbr> any amounts that are considered <a href="#employment" aria-controls="employment" class="overlay-lnk" role="button">employment income <span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a>.</p>
<p>You must provide the <abbr title="record of employment">ROE</abbr> to the worker within <strong>one</strong> of the following:</p>

<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-or cnjnctn-sm">
 <li class="cnjnctn-col">5 calendar days after the end of the pay period in which the interruption of earnings occurs</li> 
 <li class="cnjnctn-col">5 days after the worker asks for one</li> 
</ul>
