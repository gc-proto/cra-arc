<p>Once you have calculated the insurable earnings of the worker, you also <strong>have</strong> to report this amount, including the <abbr title="Employment Insurance">EI</abbr> premiums you deducted on the <abbr title="record of employment">ROE</abbr>. </p>
<p>You must provide the <abbr title="record of employment">ROE</abbr> to the worker within <strong>one</strong> of the following:</p>

<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-or cnjnctn-sm">
 <li class="cnjnctn-col">5 calendar days after the end of the contract </li> 
 <li class="cnjnctn-col">5 days after the worker asks for one</li> 
</ul>

<p>Learn more: <a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/programs/ei/ei-list/reports/roe-guide.html#h2.3-3.5">How to complete the record of employment (ROE) form</a>.</p>
