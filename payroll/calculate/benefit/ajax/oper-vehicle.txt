<p>The operating expense benefit is the benefit your employee (or a person who does not deal at arm's length with the employee) gets when the leased or owned automobile is made available for their personal use, by you or another person, because of the employee's current, previous, or intended office or employment, and you (or another person) pays for the operating expenses related to personal use (including the GST/HST and PST). The operating expense benefit only applies if there is, or would be, a standby charge benefit to the employee or a person who does not deal at arm's length with the employee.</p>
<p>If the conditions met in step 12 are met, you can calculate the benefit using an optional calculation. Otherwise, the benefit is calculated using the fixed rate.</p>
