<ul  class="fa-ul">
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> Generally provided to your employees along with salary</li>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> Generally includes meals and allowances</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div id="ajax-def2"><h5>Housing or utilities</h5> 
<ul  class="fa-ul">
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> Accommodations you provide to an employee rent-free, or for rent that is below the fair market value. Accommodations include:
<ul>
 <li>House</li>
<li>Condominium</li>
<li>Apartment</li>
<li>Room</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> Allowances towards housing costs you pay your employee</li>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> May include the cost of utilities. Utilities include:
<ul>
 <li>Electricity</li>
<li>Natural gas</li>
<li>Water</li>
<li>Telephone</li>
<li>Internet</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span class="fas fa-ban text-danger fa-li"></span> Generally, it <strong>does not</strong> include meals and transportation</li>
</ul>
</div>
