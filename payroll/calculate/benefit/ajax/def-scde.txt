<details><summary>What is a <abbr title="Self-contained domestic establishment">SCDE</abbr></summary>
<div id="ajax-scde2">
<p>A <abbr title="Self-contained domestic establishment">SCDE</abbr> is a house, an apartment, or other similar place of residence where a person usually sleeps and eats. </p>
<p>It is generally a living unit with restricted access that contains a kitchen, bathroom, and sleeping facilities. The <abbr title="Self-contained domestic establishment">SCDE</abbr> must be separate from any other living unit in the same building. </p>
