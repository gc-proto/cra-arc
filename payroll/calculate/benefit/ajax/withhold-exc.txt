<p class="mrgn-bttm-lg">The withholding and remitting requirement depends on the type of remuneration: <a href="#def-cash" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">cash&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a>, <a href="#def-noncash" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx nowrap" role="button">non-cash&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a>, or <a href="#def-nearcash" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx nowrap" role="button">near-cash&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a>.</p>
<p><strong>If the benefit is taxable</strong>, you must withhold the following deductions: </p>

<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-or cnjnctn-sm"> 
  <li class="cnjnctn-col">
    <h3 class="mrgn-tp-sm text-muted h4">Non-cash and near-cash<span class="wb-inv">: Option 1</span></h3>
    <p class="mrgn-bttm-0">Withhold: </p>
                     <ul class="fa-ul mrgn-bttm-lg">
                        <li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> Income tax</li>
                         <li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> CPP</li>
                         <li><span class="fas fa-times text-danger fa-li"></span> EI <small class="text-muted">(do not withhold <strong>unless cash</strong> earnings were <strong>also paid in the pay period</strong>)</small></li>
                     </ul>
                     <p class="mrgn-bttm-0">Remit:</p>
                      <ul class="fa-ul">
                        <li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span>GST/HST <small class="text-muted">in certain situations</small></li>
			
						
                     </ul>
  </li>
  <li class="cnjnctn-col">
    <h3 class="mrgn-tp-sm text-muted h4">Cash<span class="wb-inv">: Option 2</span></h3>
    <p class="mrgn-bttm-0">Withhold: </p>
                      <ul class="fa-ul mrgn-bttm-lg">
                       <li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> Income tax</li>
                         <li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> CPP</li>
                         <li><span class="fas fa-check fa-li text-success"></span> EI</li>
                     </ul>
                     <p class="mrgn-bttm-0">Do not remit:</p>
                     <ul class="fa-ul">
                         <li><span class="fas fa-times text-danger fa-li"></span> GST/HST <small class="text-muted">(do not remit)</small></li>				
					 </ul>
  </li>
</ul>
<p>The amounts must be included in the pay period they were received or enjoyed.</p>
