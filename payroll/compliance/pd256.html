What is a PD256 letter

The CRA may amend previously filed slips to correct a reporting error or may add slips when a slip was not originally filed. A PD256 letter is sent to you if the CRA needs to provide you with amended or additional T4, T4A, or T4RSP slips.

Before you receive the PD256 letter, the CRA may either have issued to you a:

  • Pensionable and insurable earnings review (PIER)

    Learn more: Pensionable and insurable earnings review (PIER)

  • Letter of explanation

What to do if you received a PD256 letter

You must distribute the amended or additional slips to the affected workers.

Keep a copy of the slip for your records, or update your records with the information from the amended or additional slips.

Learn more: Distribute the slips

Impact on taxes and benefits for workers

As a result of these corrections, changes to the worker’s previously filed personal income tax and benefit returns will be required.

If the worker receives an amended or additional slip from their employer or a payer, the worker must determine if:

  • The CRA reassessed their return
  • They must send a request to reassess their previously filed return

Learn more: Reassessments after payroll compliance reviews

What if you disagree with the slips attached to the PD256 letter

If you disagree with the CRA amended or additional slips, to avoid processing delays, send all of the following required documents within 30 days of the date of your response:

  • Letter to explain why you disagree
  • Amended slips for your affected workers.

How to send your documents

If you received the PD256 letter as a result of:

  • Pensionable and insurable earnings review (PIER)

    You can send using one of the following methods:

  • Letter of explanation

    You can send using one of the following methods:

    1. File the amended T4 slips

      For faster service, file amended T4 slips for your affected employees electronically to avoid the delays in processing paper slips.

      If you file the amended T4 slips electronically, you will receive a confirmation number. Use this number when sending your required documents.

      Learn more: How to file - File payroll information returns (slips and summaries).

    2. Send your response

      You can send your response online, by fax or by mail. You have to include your confirmation number if you filed the amended T4 slips electronically.

      Online - For faster service, send your response using our online services

      Send all your required documents using our online services:

      1. Sign in to your CRA account
      2. Click “Submit documents” in the Correspondence menu and then click “Submit documents” at the bottom of the page
      3. Click “Start” and answer “No” to the question “Do you have a case or reference number?”
      4. Select “Payroll notices, statements, and vouchers” from the Topic menu
      5. Select “Other payroll accounts notice(s)”
      6. Select the “Program account number” associated to the documents you are submitting and click “Next”
      7. Click “+ Attachments” to attach your required documents and use the “Browse” button to choose the file(s) you want to attach from your device (if more than one file is being added, each must have a unique name)
      8. Enter the confirmation number(s) you received when you filed your amended T4 slips and that you are responding as a result of a PD256 letter in the blank field “Provide a brief description of your document” and click “Upload file(s)”
      9. If you want to attach more documents, click “+Attachments” again and repeat steps 2-3 and when all your documents are added, click “Next”
      10. Review and click “Submit”

      Keep the confirmation page for your records. You cannot use the confirmation number on this page to send additional documents.
