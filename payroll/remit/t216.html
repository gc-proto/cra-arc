T216 - Accelerated remitter notification and questionnaire

The CRA may send you a T216 - Accelerated remitter notification and questionnaire if you are a regular or quarterly remitter and your average monthly withholding amount (AMWA)  was at least $25,000 in the previous year.

What is a T216

The T216 is used to determine if the remitter type for your payroll account will change to an accelerated remitter for the next calendar year.

Example

An employer is a regular remitter who had an AMWA of $30,000 in 2023. The employer will receive a T216 in July 2024. The response to that T216 will determine if the employer will be an accelerated remitter in 2025.

When is the T216 sent

The accelerated remitter notification and questionnaire (T216) is sent in July.

What methods the CRA uses to send you the T216

The T216 will be sent to you in the following methods:

  • Electronically when you are registered for My Business Account (MyBA) or Represent a Client (RAC) using the online services at CRA sign-in services.
  • Mail whether or not you are registered for the electronic services

When do you need to respond to the T216

You have to respond to the T216 by the last Friday of July.

Once the CRA has reviewed your response, if your remitter type changes, they will send you additional information in November on when and how to remit (pay) in the following year.

What if you do not respond to the T216

If you do not respond to the T216, a notice will be sent to inform you that the remitter type for your payroll account was changed to an accelerated remitter.

How to respond to the T216

You can respond by sending your completed T216 using one of the following methods:

  • My Business Account (MyBA) or Represent a Client (RAC) using the online services at CRA sign-in services
  • By mail using the return mail address on the form
