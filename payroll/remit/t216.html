Accelerated remitter notification and T216 questionnaire

The CRA may send you an accelerated remitter notification if you are a regular or quarterly remitter and your average monthly withholding amount (AMWA)  was at least $25,000 in the previous year. This notification will include a T216 questionnaire.

What is a T216 questionnaire

The T216 questionnaire is used to determine if the remitter type for your payroll account will change to an accelerated remitter for the next calendar year.

Example

An employer is a regular remitter who had an AMWA of $30,000 in 2022. The employer will receive a T216 questionnaire in July 2023. The response to that T216 questionnaire will determine if the employer will be an accelerated remitter in 2024.

How often is the T216 questionnaire sent

The accelerated remitter notification, including the T216 questionnaire is sent in July.

What methods the CRA uses to send you the T216 questionnaire

The T216 questionnaire will be sent to you in the following methods:

  • Electronically when you are registered for My Business Account (MyBA) or Represent a Client (RAC) using the online services at CRA sign-in services.
  • Mail whether or not you are registered for the electronic services

When to respond to the T216 questionnaire

You have to respond to the T216 questionnaire by the last Friday of July.

Once the CRA reviewed your response, they will send you additional information in November on when and how to remit (pay) in the following year.

What if you do not respond to the T216 questionnaire

If you do not respond to the T216 questionnaire, a notice will be sent to you to inform you that the remitter type for your payroll account was changed to an accelerated remitter.

How to respond to a T216 questionnaire

You can respond by sending your completed T216 questionnaire using one of the following method:

  • My Business Account (MyBA) or Represent a Client (RAC) using the online services at CRA sign-in services
  • By mail using the return mail address on the form
