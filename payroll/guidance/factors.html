Determine if the worker is an employee or self-employed

Content has been updated for clarity, completeness and plain language, including additional examples and legislative references.

You need to consider multiple factors to determine if the worker is an employee or is self-employed.

Unless the valid contract states differently, the factors are different if the contract is formed:

  • Contract formed in a province or territory not in Quebec

    Applies to employment performed in and outside Canada, where employment is in:

    • AB
    • BC
    • MB
    • NB
    • NL
    • NT
    • NS
    • NU
    • ON
    • PE
    • SK
    • YT

  • Contract formed in the province of Quebec

    Applies to employment performed in and outside Canada, where employment is in:

    • QC
