Determine if the persons are related

You must determine if the persons are considered related under the Income Tax Act before you can determine whether individuals or entities are dealing at arm’s length.

Type of relationships

Select the type of relationships to determine if the persons are considered related.

  • Two individuals
  • A person (or related group of person) and a corporation
  • Two corporations
  • A person and a partnership
  • A person and a trust
