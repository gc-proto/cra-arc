Determine if persons are related and if they are dealing at arm's length

Use this guidance to help you understand how to determine if persons are related and if they are dealing at arm's length.

What is a person

Person includes:

  • An individual
  • A corporation
  • Any entity included in subsection 149(1) (for example: municipalities, Crown corporations, registered charities, universities, athletic associations, labour organizations, non-profit organizations and trusts)
  • Heirs, executors, liquidators of a succession, administrators or other legal representatives of an entity included in subsection 149(1)
  • A partnership, for GST/HST purposes, but not for income tax purposes
