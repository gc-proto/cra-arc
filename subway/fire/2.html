When to file

Determine the filing due date

The filing due date is based on information return type. Refer to the guides listed below full details:

Listing of guides
  • AGR-1, Statement of Farm-Support Payments
  • NR4, Statement of Amounts Paid or Credited to Non-Residents of Canada
  • T3, Statement of Trusts Income Allocations and Designations
  • T3D, Income Tax Return for Deferred Profit Sharing Plan (DPSP) or Revoked DPSP
  • T3GR, Group Income Tax and Information Return for RRSP, RRIF, RESP, or RDSP Trusts
  • T3P, Employees’ Pension Plan Income Tax Return
  • T3PRP, T3 Pooled Registered Pension Pan Tax Return
  • T3RI, Registered Investment Income Tax Return
  • T4, Statement of Remuneration Paid
  • T4A, Statement of Pension, Retirement, Annuity, and Other Income
  • T4A-NR, Statement of Fees, Commissions, or Other Amounts Paid to Non-Residents for Services Rendered in Canada
  • T1204, Government Service Contract Payments
  • T4RIF, Statement of Income From a Registered Retirement Income Fund
  • T4RSP, Statement of Registered Retirement Savings Plan Income
  • T5, Statement of Investment Income
  • T5007, Statement of Benefits
  • T5008, Statement of Securities Transactions
  • T5018, Statement of Contract Payments
  • T4A(OAS), Statement of Old Age Security
  • T4A(P), Statement of Canada Pension/Quebec Pension Plan Benefits
  • T4E, Statement of Employment Insurance Benefits
  • SAFER, Manitoba Shelter Allowance for Elderly Renters (Rent Assist)
  • RRSP, Contribution Receipt Filing
  • T215, Exempt from Certification Past Service Pension Adjustment (PSPA)
  • TFSA, Tax Free Savings Account
  • T4FHSA, Tax Free First Home Savings Account
  • T1061, Canadian Amateur Athletic Trust Group Information Return
  • T5013, FIN Partnership Financial Return
  • T5013, Partnership Information Return
  • T661, Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED) Expenditures Claim
  • PRPP, Pooled Registered Pension Plan Information Return
  • RRSP and RRIF, Non-Qualified Investments Information Return
  • Part XVIII Information Return, International Exchange of Information on Financial Accounts
  • Part XIX Information Return, International Exchange of Information on Financial Accounts
  • Worksheet T2000, Calculation of Tax on Agreements to Acquire Shares (Section 207.1(5) of the Income Tax Act)
  • T2202, Tuition and enrolment certificate

What if you file your information return late

If you file your information return past the due date you may be charged a penalty.

Refer to: Late-filing penalties for information returns
