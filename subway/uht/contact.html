Online

Use your CRA My Account to:

  • update your direct deposit information

By phone

Call the CRA for more information or help by phone.

Individuals

Before you call

Have the following information ready if you’re calling about your account:

  • tax identifier number:
    • social insurance number (SIN)
    • international tax number (ITN)
    • temporary tax number (TTN)
  • full name and date of birth
  • complete address
  • assessed tax return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document, or be signed in to CRA My Account
