Sign in to your account for self-service

Sign in to your account to view and update your information.

  • Using your CRA My Account: Option 1

    Use your CRA My Account to:

    • get tax credit information
    • send new documentation using "Submit documents"
    • and more, refer to the full list of online services available to you
    Sign in to My Account Register

  • Using your My Service Canada Account: Option 2

    If you don't have CRA My Account, you can still access your CRA My Account by first signing into your My Service Canada Account and then toggling over:

    1. Sign in to your My Service Canada Account
    2. Select "Access CRA"
    3. Select "I agree" on the Registration and Authentication page to transfer directly to your CRA My Account
Call us

Call the CRA for more information or help by phone.

Before you call

To verify your identity, you'll need your:

  • social insurance number (SIN)
  • full name and date of birth
  • complete address
  • assessed tax return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document, or be signed in to CRA My Account
If you're calling on behalf of someone else

If you're calling on behalf of someone else, they must be present on the call to give their consent, or you must be authorized to call on their behalf.

For details: Authorize a representative
