Multigenerational home renovation tax credit (MGHRTC)

The Multigenerational home renovation tax credit (MGHRTC) is a refundable credit to assist with the cost of renovating a home to create a secondary unit for either:

  • a senior (65 years old, or older)
  • an adult (18 to 64 years old) who is eligible for the disability tax credit
