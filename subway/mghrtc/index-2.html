Multigenerational home renovation tax credit (MGHRTC)

Status: Available starting on 2023 tax returns

The new Multigenerational home renovation tax credit (MGHRTC) is a refundable credit that can be claimed on your individual tax return.

If you are eligible, you can claim a deduction credit for certain renovation expenses involved in creating a secondary unit. The secondary unit must be self-contained with a private entrance, kitchen, bathroom facilities and sleeping area, and enable a senior or an adult who is eligible for the disability tax credit to live with a qualifying relative.

The credit is 15% of the eligible renovation expenses, to a maximum of $50,000. The maximum MGHRTC is $7,500.
