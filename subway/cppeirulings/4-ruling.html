Our review and decision

Steps the CRA officer will take after receiving a request for a ruling

  1. An authorized officer contacts the worker and the payer to discuss the working arrangement. Sometimes the officer has to ask for supporting documents. These could include employment contracts and agreements, invoices, logs, work schedules, pay stubs, and cancelled cheques.
  2. The officer will decide whether the worker is an employee working in pensionable or insurable employment for purposes of the CPP, the EI Act, or both.
  3. The officer will send a ruling letter to the worker and the payer, and if applicable to their representatives, to inform them of the decision. The officer will also notify Service Canada or one of CRA’s programs if they initiated the request.
