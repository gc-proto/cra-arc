Our review and decision

Steps the CRA officer will take

  1. An authorized officer contacts the worker and the payer to discuss the working arrangement. Sometimes the officer has to ask for supporting documents. These include such things as employment contracts and agreements, invoices, logs, work schedules, pay stubs, and cancelled cheques.
  2. The officer will decide whether the worker is an employee working in pensionable or insurable employment for purposes of the CPP, the EI Act or both.
  3. The officer will then send a ruling letter to the worker and the payer or their representatives, if appropriate, to inform them of the decision.
