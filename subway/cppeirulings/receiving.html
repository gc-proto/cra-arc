If you receive a CPP/EI ruling

If you receive a CPP/EI ruling from the CRA, it is because a request for a ruling was initiated by one of the following:

  • A payer
  • A worker
  • Service Canada, for the administration of the EI program
  • The CRA, for the administration of some of its programs

A Canada Pension Plan and employment insurance (CPP/EI) ruling is an official decision made by an authorized officer of the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). The ruling confirms whether a worker is an employee or is self-employed and whether the worker's employment is pensionable, insurable, or both.

For possible implications of a ruling and what to do if you disagree, go to: After a ruling has been made
