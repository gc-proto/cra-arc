How to make a request

To ask for a CPP/EI ruling, you can:

  • Log in to My Business Account, if you are a payer, and select “Request a CPP/EI Ruling”
  • Log in to My Account, if you are a worker or a payer, and select “Request a CPP/EI Ruling” 
  • Ask your authorized representative to request a ruling for you – they can log in to Represent a Client and select “Request a CPP/EI Ruling”
  • Write a letter or fill out Form CPT1, Request for a CPP/EI Ruling – Employee or self-employed?, and mail it to the CPP/EI Rulings Division at the tax services office in the province or territory of your residence or place of business – see Form CPT1 for the mailing addresses
