What is the GenAI Chatbot pilot

The CRA is testing a Generative Artificial Intelligence (Gen AI) Chatbot to improve our digital services and help users self-serve more easily.

Starting on March 31st, 2025, the GenAI Chatbot will be available 24/7 to answer questions relating to personal income tax, as well as information relating to accessing a CRA online account.

You will be offered a survey after using the GenAI Chatbot, we encourage you to take part. Your feedback will help to improve and expand the service in the future.

Before you begin

Privacy notice

Please do not share any personal details like your name, address, Social Insurance Number (SIN), date of birth, or financial information while using this Chatbot. The GenAI Chatbot is not designed to securely handle personal or sensitive information. During your interaction with the GenAI Chatbot, your questions may be temporarily retained to enhance your Chatbot experience. Your personal information, chat history, or other interactions are not stored or used. For more details on privacy, please refer to our Privacy Statement.

Handling of personal information

Users are advised not to enter personal information when interacting with the GenAI Chatbot. If a user enters personal information during their interaction, the CRA will check to ensure it aligns with section 4 of the Privacy Act. Any unauthorized information will be promptly deleted to reduce privacy risks. The GenAI Chatbot can usually identify questions containing personal information, which may help in deleting such data.

Important information for users

The information provided by the GenAI Chatbot is based on Canada.ca webpages and should not be taken as professional, legal, or medical advice. Information from the GenAI Chatbot may not be fully accurate or meet your specific needs. Always use your best judgment and consult human professionals or other online sources before making decisions based on this information. You use this information at your own risk.

How to use the GenAI Chatbot

Follow the steps below to chat with the GenAI Chatbot.

  1. Open the Chatbot

    You will find the GenAI Chatbot widget in the lower right corner of this page. To start, select the Chatbot widget to open a chat session.

  2. Ask your question

    Type your question in the message box, and select the icon or Enter to send.

    To help the GenAI Chatbot find the information you need:

    • Ask your question in complete sentences
    • Avoid using slang or jargon
    • Describe your situation without sharing personal information
    • Focus on one topic per question

    If you were unable to get the answer you were looking for:

    • Ensure your question is related to Personal Income Taxes, or the CRA account registration and sign-in information.
    • Try rephrasing your question.

We want your feedback

Sharing your experience will help us improve our services. Please take a moment to complete the CRA GenAI Chatbot survey. The CRA will monitor survey feedback to improve and expand the service in the future.

You can request the survey by typing ‘survey’ in the message box or click the survey link at the end of your session.
