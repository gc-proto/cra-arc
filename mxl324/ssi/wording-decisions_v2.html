Wording reference for the CRA account

Follow these guidelines to preserve consistency across our Canada.ca pages when referring to the CRA account.

On this page

  • Links
  • Steps for signing in
  • CRA account terms

Links

Sign-in link

Use the possessive "your" as the user already has an account.

  • English: Option 1

    "Sign in to your CRA account"

    • Link to: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/cra-login-services.html

  • French: Option 2

    "Se connecter à son compte de l'ARC" or "Connectez-vous à votre compte de l'ARC"

    • Link to: https://www.canada.ca/fr/agence-revenu/services/services-electroniques/services-ouverture-session-arc.html
Registration link

Do not use the possessive "your" as the user does not have a CRA account yet.

If used with a green button, the link should be on the same line, after the green button.

  • English: Option 1

    "Register for a CRA account"

    • Link to: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/cra-login-services/register-cra-sign-in-services.html
  • French: Option 2

    "S'inscrire à un compte de l'ARC"

    • Link to: https://www.canada.ca/fr/agence-revenu/services/services-electroniques/services-ouverture-session-arc/inscrire-services-ouverture-session-arc.html
Green button link

Use the same text from the blue sign-in button at the top of many of our pages.

  • English: Option 1

    "CRA sign in"

    • Link to: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/cra-login-services.html

  • French: Option 2

    "Se connecter à l'ARC"

    • Link to: https://www.canada.ca/fr/agence-revenu/services/services-electroniques/services-ouverture-session-arc.html

Steps for signing in

Step 1

If there is a green button above the instructions, do not add a link to step 1.

If there is no green button, link the whole sentence.

  • English: Option 1

    Use "Sign in to your CRA account"

    • If no green button, link to: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/cra-login-services.html

  • French: Option 2

    Use "Connectez-vous à votre compte de l'ARC"

    • If no green button, link to: https://www.canada.ca/fr/agence-revenu/services/services-electroniques/services-ouverture-session-arc.html
Step 2

Capitalize and bold the account name even if it is not a proper noun as it is a selection (bold) and what the user will actually see on the Welcome page (capitalization).

Do not add a link in step 2.

For My Account, choose either:

  • English: Option 1

    • "Select your Individual account"
    • "Select your Individual account to access My Account"

  • French: Option 2

    • "Choisissez votre compte Particulier."
    • "Choisissez votre compte Particulier pour accéder à Mon dossier."
For My Business Account, choose either:

  • English: Option 1

    • "Select your Business account"
    • "Select your Business account to access My Business Account"

  • French: Option 2

    • "Choisissez votre compte Entreprise."
    • "Choisissez votre compte Entreprise pour accéder à Mon dossier d'entreprise."
For Represent a Client, choose either:

  • English: Option 1

    • "Select your Representative account"
    • "Select your Representative account to access Represent a Client"

  • French: Option 2

    • "Choisissez votre compte Représentant."
    • "Choisissez votre compte Représentant pour accéder à Représenter un client."

CRA account glossary

Account

Use "account" when referring to the 3 types of accounts: Individual/Business/Representative.

Always capitalize and bold the name of the account (Individual/Business/Representative) and use lowercase for the word "account" in both languages.

Always use lowercase for the CRA account.

  • English: Option 1

    Use "Individual/Business/Representative account"

  • French: Option 2

    Use "compte Particulier/Entreprise/Représentant"

    • In other contexts, we can also use "compte de représentant" and "compte d'entreprise" as these are buttons in the portal.
Example

English

  • Select your Representative account from the Welcome page to access Represent a Client.

French

  • Choisissez votre compte Représentant à la page d’accueil pour accéder à Représenter un client.
My Account, My Business Account, Represent a Client<

You can use My Account, My Business Account or Represent a Client. In steps or when providing navigation instructions, refer to Steps for signing in.

Do not use possessive pronouns when referring to the branded accounts (for example, do not use "In your My Account").

Example: Using the branded account names

English

  • If you want to access My Account on behalf of someone else (including friends and family members), you must be their authorized representative and use Represent a Client from your CRA account.

French

  • Si vous voulez accéder à Mon dossier au nom d'une autre personne (y compris un ami ou un membre de votre famille), vous devez être son représentant autorisé et utiliser Représenter un client à partir de votre compte de l’ARC pour accéder à ses renseignements.
Portal

The word "portal" may be used to refer to the CRA account only, not for the Individual/Business/Representative accounts

Example

English

  • The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) has a portal, CRA account, that provides access to My Account, My Business Account, and Represent a Client and the services within.

French

  • L’Agence du revenu du Canada (ARC) dispose d’un portail, le compte de l’ARC, qui donne accès à Mon dossier, Mon dossier d’entreprise et Représenter un client, ainsi qu’aux services qu’ils offrent.
Sign in spelling

  • English: Option 1

    Use the verb "sign in" and the noun or adjective "sign-in" (hyphenated)

  • French: Option 2

    Use the verb "Connectez-vous" or the noun "Connexion". Do not use "ouverture de session".

Examples: “Sign-in” as a noun vs. a verb

Using the verb "sign in"

  • English: Sign in or register to access My Account, My Business Account or Represent a Client.
  • French: Connectez-vous ou inscrivez-vous pour accéder à Mon dossier, Mon dossier d'entreprise ou Représenter un client auprès de l'ARC.

Using the noun "sign-in"

  • English: The CRA has introduced a simplified sign-in process to make it easier to access My Account, My Business Account, and Represent a Client with a single sign-in.
  • French: L’ARC a mis en place un processus de connexion unique afin de simplifier l’accès à Mon dossier, Mon dossier d’entreprise et Représenter un client.
Sign in services

Use when talking about all CRA sign-in services (including CRA account, Candidate profile, etc.).

  • English: Option 1

    Use "CRA account and other sign-in services"

  • French: Option 2

    Use "Compte de l'ARC et autres services d’ouverture de session"

Example: Referring to CRA sign-in services

English

  • The CRA account and other sign-in services are available 21 hours a day.

French

  • Le compte de l’ARC et les autres services d’ouverture de session sont disponibles 21 heures par jour.
Service

Use "service" when referring to what is available within each account.

Example: Using “service”

English

  • Before you complete your identity verification, you will have limited access to services in My Account.

French

  • Avant de procéder à la vérification de votre identité, vous aurez un accès limité aux services de Mon dossier.
Tile

Use "tile" (encadré in French) when referring to the boxes on the Welcome page.

Example: Referring to Welcome page tiles

English

  • Step 1: Sign in to your CRA account
  • Step 2: Select the mail icon in the Business tile

French

  • Étape 1 : Connectez-vous à votre compte de l’ARC.
  • Étape 2 : Choisissez l’icône de courrier (enveloppe) dans l’encadré Entreprise.
