After you file

For detailed information, refer to:

  • After you file a Part XVIII or Part XIX information return

After filing a Part XVIII or Part XIX information return, you will receive a submission number which confirms receipt of the information.  This submission number does not, however, confirm that the return was processed. To confirm your return was processed you need to log into your My Business Account or Represent a Client account.

Audit process at a glance

Audits may be a combination of onsite and offsite work and will cover Part XVIII and Part XIX of the ITA as well as testing of record keeping and pre-existing account requirements.

Should your financial institution be selected for an audit, a CRA auditor will:

  • Contact the appropriate person:
    • The authorized representative of the RZ program account that the Part XVIII and Part XIX information returns are filed under
    • A director if there is no authorized representative for the RZ program account

    Financial institutions must ensure that their authorized representative information is correct and up to date. Financial institutions may choose to:

    • Use the same authorized representative as other program accounts
      • If this is the case, that individual's authorization must be updated to include the RZ program account the Part XVIII and Part XIX information returns are filed under
    • Designate someone else as the authorized representative for the RZ account by modifying or adding an authorized representative
  • Send a formal letter to the financial institution
  • Inform the individual of the audit period, the audit process, and other relevant details
  • Review the requested documentation and communicate any concerns with the financial institution
  • Send a Compliance Report and Action Plan (if deficiencies are found) to the financial institution
