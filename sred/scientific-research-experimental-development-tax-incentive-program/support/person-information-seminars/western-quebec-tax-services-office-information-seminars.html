Scientific research and experimental development (SR&ED) tax incentives

SR&ED webinars for Quebec

The Western Quebec Tax Services Office serves businesses that conduct scientific research and experimental development (SR&ED) work in Quebec.

Free information webinars provide a general overview of the SR&ED program, and explain the eligibility criteria, what expenditures are eligible, and how to file an SR&ED claim.

The webinar will focus on Quebec, however anyone interested in the SR&ED program may attend.
