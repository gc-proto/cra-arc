How to prepare your claim

Follow the steps below to prepare your SR&ED tax incentives claim youself.

Steps

  1. Get ready to claim

    Understand how to claim, resources you’ll need, due dates, and which records to save

  2. Group work into SR&ED projects

    Review what an SR&ED project is and how to group your eligible work into projects

  3. Report your expenditures

    Review how to determine your SR&ED expenditures and use the SR&ED Expenditures Claim form

  4. Calculate allowable expenditures

    Determine the expenditures you may claim, including salary or wages, materials, contracts, overhead, and third-party payments

  5. Calculate pool of deductibles

    Determine your pool of deductible SR&ED expenditures

  6. Calculate qualified expenditures

    Determine the SR&ED expenditures that qualify for an investment tax credit (ICT)

  7. Claim investment tax credit (ICT)

    Learn who can earn an ITC, review rates, and find out if your ITC may be refundable
