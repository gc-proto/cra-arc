Scientific research and experimental development (SR&ED) tax incentives

Support services and tools

On this page

  • Before you start your claim
  • After you submit your claim

Before you submit a claim

Webinars

SR&ED information webinars, provided in English and French, can give a general overview of the SR&ED Program, including eligibility criteria, allowable expenditures, and how to prepare a claim for SR&ED tax incentives.

Self-Assessment Learning Tool 9SALT)

This online self-assessment tool helps you determine if your work is eligible for SR&ED tax incentives and what information you need to complete your claim. It can also estimate the investment tax credit (ITC) you may be able to get.

Pre-claim consulation

The Pre-claim consultation service can identify if there is scientific research and experimental development (SR&ED) work in any of your projects before you submit a claim for SR&ED tax incentives.

Outreach program

The Outreach program offers a variety of education products to businesses conducting SR&ED work in Canada. This includes giving presentations, speaking at events, and providing products tailored to your industry.

After you submit a claim

First-time claimant advisory service (FTCAS)

The First-Time Claimant Advisory Service (FTCAS) is a mandatory service that provides selected SR&ED claimants with recommendations and guidance for future claims.

SR&ED service visit

The SR&ED service visit is a voluntary service that gives claimants an opportunity to discuss any aspect of the SR&ED program with a CRA representative. The virtual visit is initiated by the CRA and can provide guidance for future claims.

Contact us

If you have any questions about SR&ED tax incentives, call the coordinating tax services office for your region.
