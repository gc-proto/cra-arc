Review your claim

Claimants are responsible for the information in their claim. Whether you prepare the claim yourself, or it is done by someone else, we recommend checking that your claim is complete, accurate, and supported by evidence before you submit it.

A complete SR&ED claim must include:

  • Form T661, SR&ED Expenditures Claim (current version), in which you
    • indicate if you are using the traditional or proxy method to report your expenditures
    • describe the scientific or technological uncertainty you tried to overcome
  • Schedule T2SCH31 or Form T2038(IND) to claim an ITC
  • If applicable, Forms T1145, T1146, T1174, and/or T1263, and any attachments

For more information on required forms, review in the filing policy: Prescribed forms for an SR&ED claim

Submit your claim

Reporting deadline

The SR&ED reporting deadline is 12 months after your income tax filing due date for the year.

We recommend that you submit your SR&ED claim before the reporting deadline and together with your income tax return or information return.

For more information: Filing due dates

Methods to use/ Submit electronically or by paper

Submit your SR&ED claim to the CRA with your original income tax return for the year or with an amended return.

As with your tax return, you may submit your claim electronically or by paper.

For more information: Income tax
