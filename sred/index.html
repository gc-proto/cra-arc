Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED) tax incentives

The Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED) tax incentives are intended to encourage business to conduct research and development in Canada.

Corporations, individuals, trusts, and members of a partnership may be able to claim SR&ED tax incentives for the year if their work is eligible.

To benefit from the incentives, you must link your eligible work to expenditures you can claim. Then you may deduct the expenditures against your income or earn an income tax credit (ITC).

Submit your SR&ED claim with your income tax return for the year.
