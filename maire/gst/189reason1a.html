Complete the form

Apply for a rebate under reason code 1A by filling out Form GST 189, General Application for GST/HST rebates.

Follow the instructions on the form to fill out parts A, B, C, D (section 1), E (if applicable), G (if applicable) and H. Make sure to do all of the following:

  • Check the box for reason code 1A in Part B
  • List all receipts and invoices separately in Part C for the period covered. If there is not enough room in Part C to enter all the information needed, also fill out Form GST288, Supplement to Forms GST189 and GST498
  • Add all the amounts you listed in Part C and on Form GST288, if you used it, under "GST/HST", and enter the total at the bottom of the column. Enter this amount in Section 1 of Part D
  • Sign your rebate application in Part H. If you have authorized a representative using Form GST507, Third-Party Authorization and Cancellation of Authorization for GST/HST Rebates, they may sign on your behalf
