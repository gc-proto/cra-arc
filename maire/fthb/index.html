First-time home buyers’ (FTHB) GST/HST rebate

Status: Applications are open

Processing times

The CRA is updating filing systems to process applications where the purchase agreement was signed between March 20, 2025, and May 26, 2025, and applications for the Ontario first-time home buyers' rebate. The CRA will begin processing these applications once the filing system updates are completed.

For more information, refer to: When your application will be processed

The first-time home buyers’ (FTHB) GST/HST rebate:

  • Eliminates the GST (or federal part of the HST) for first-time home buyers on a new home valued up to $1 million
  • Allows first-time home buyers to reduce the amount of tax they pay on a new home valued between $1 million and $1.5 million

As a first-time home buyer, you may be eligible for the FTHB GST/HST rebate in addition to the existing GST/HST new housing rebate. Where both rebates apply, the FTHB GST/HST rebate acts as a top up to the existing GST/HST new housing rebate.

In Ontario, first-time home buyers may also be eligible for a rebate of some of the 8% provincial part of the HST. Similar to the FTHB GST/HST rebate, the Ontario FTHB rebate acts as a top up to the existing Ontario new housing rebate.

Learn more: GST/HST new housing rebate

For technical enquiries, contact GST/HST Rulings at 1-800-959-8287.
