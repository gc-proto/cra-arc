How to apply

How you apply for the rebate will depend on whether you purchased your home from a builder or built your home yourself. Choose one of the following for details.

  • Home purchased from a builder: Option 1

    If you purchased a home from a builder or bought a share of the capital stock of a co-op, use the following forms to apply for this rebate:

    Recover the GST or federal part of the HST
    Form GST190, GST/HST New Housing Rebate Application for Houses Purchased from a Builder
    Recover the provincial part of the HST (if your house is in Ontario)
    Form RC7190-ON, GST190 Ontario Rebate Schedule

    If you and the builder agree that the builder will pay or credit you with the amount of the rebate, the builder must file your application with the CRA.

  • Owner-built home: Option 2

    If you built or substantially renovated your own home or hired someone to do so, use the following forms to apply for this rebate:

    To help you determine the amount of the GST/HST you paid during the construction
    Form GST191-WS, Construction Summary Worksheet
    To recover the GST or federal part of the HST
    Form GST191, GST/HST New Housing Rebate Application for Owner-Built Houses
    To recover the provincial part of the HST (if your house is in Ontario)
    Form RC7191-ON, GST191 Ontario Rebate Schedule

How to submit

Online:

  1. Sign into your CRA account
  2. Select your individual account
  3. Select File a GST/HST rebate under "More services"

By mail

If you can’t submit your application online, you can send it by mail.

  1. Fill out your application forms
  2. Send the completed forms to the address on the form

For detailed guidance on deadlines on filling out these forms, the deadline for filing and where to send your application, refer to: Guide RC4028, GST/HST New Housing Rebate
