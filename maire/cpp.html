How to ask for a ruling

The fastest way to ask for a CPP/EI ruling is through your CRA account.

CRA sign in

After you sign in to your CRA account, you can:

  • Access My Business Account, if you are a payer, and select “Request a CPP/EI Ruling”
  • Access My Account, if you are a worker or a payer, and select “Request a CPP/EI Ruling”
  • Ask your authorized representative to request a ruling for you – they can access Represent a Client and select “Request a CPP/EI Ruling”

If you do not want to ask for a ruling online, you can write a letter or fill out Form CPT1, Request for a CPP/EI Ruling – Employee or self-employed. Mail the completed form to the CPP/EI Rulings Division at the tax services office in the province or territory of your residence or place of business – see Form CPT1 for the mailing addresses.
