Benefits, credits and support

Benefits being promoted title

Every dollar counts to keep up with the bills, or to help with the cost of raising a family. A little bit extra can make a difference. Federal benefits, credits, and tax programs can help you make ends meet. Find out if you qualify.

Canada Child Benefit (CCB)

File your 2019 income tax return to continue your CCB payments. If your 2019 return is not assessed by early September 2020, your payments will stop in October 2020 and you will have to repay the amounts you received since July 2020.

Home Buyers' Plan (HBP)

The Home Buyers' Plan (HBP) is a program that allows you to withdraw from your registered retirement savings plans (RRSPs) to buy or build a qualifying home for yourself or for a related person with a disability.

Canada training credit (CTC)

The budget proposes a new refundable tax credit called the Canada training credit available for eligible tuition and other fees paid for courses taken in 2020 and subsequent taxation years.

Canada workers benefit (CWB)

The Canada workers benefit (CWB) is a refundable tax credit that provides tax relief for eligible low-income individuals and families who are in the workforce.

GST/HST credit

File your 2019 income tax return to continue your GST/HST credit payments. If your 2019 return is not assessed by early September 2020, your payments will stop in October 2020 and you will have to repay the amounts you received since July 2020.

Climate action incentive (CAI)

The Government of Canada has introduced the new climate action incentive (CAI) payment. If you are a resident of Saskatchewan, Manitoba, Ontario or Alberta, you can claim it when you file your 2019 income tax and benefit return.

COVID-19 Emergency benefits

TFinancial support is available to Canadians during the COVID-19 pandemic, including new benefits and credits and changes to existing ones.
