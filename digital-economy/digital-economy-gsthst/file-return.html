Complete and file a return

Complete and file a simplified GST/HST

During the reporting period for your business, you will have to complete and file your return.

Your reporting period under the simplified GST/HST will be a calendar quarter. Your return for the reporting is required to be filed within one month after the end of the reporting period.

Net tax calculation

Under the simplified GST/HST, your net tax calculation for a reporting period will generally only include the GST/HST that is collected or that becomes collectible during the reporting period and certain net tax adjustments. You will not be entitled to claim input tax credits for the GST/HST paid or payable on your business inputs, but you will be able to claim certain net tax deductions.

The simplified GST/HST return that will have fewer lines to complete than the standard GST/HST return. Generally, your net tax for each reporting period would be calculated as the difference between the following two amounts (A-B) as follows:

A: Line 105 of the return

  • GST/HST that becomes collected or collectible on your taxable supplies of short-term accommodation situated in Canada and Canadian accommodation related supplies during the reporting period

+

  • Amounts you recover as GST/HST debts during the reporting for which you had claimed a bad debt adjustment

B: Line 108 of the return

  • Amounts you may deduct for an excess GST/HST amount that you refund to a person, or credit or adjust in favour of a person, during the reporting period

+

  • Amounts you may deduct for GST/HST relating to bad debts you write-off during the reporting period

If the net tax calculation results in a positive amount, that is the amount of net tax that you must report in your return and remit (pay) to us.

If the calculation results in a negative amount, that is the amount of the net tax refund that you can claim in your return.

If you are a registered distribution platform operator, you may also be eligible to claim input tax credits in respect of the tax paid on the importation of goods by non-registered vendors who import their goods into Canada to be sold through your platform. In order to so, you would be required to obtain satisfactory evidence from the vendor that the tax in respect of the importation has been paid.

If you are an accommodation platform operator and have registered for the simplified GST/HST, you may also apply for authorization to determine your net tax for a reporting period in a qualifying foreign currency.

If you are filing a simplified GST/HST, you can only your return electronically. You can do so using the GST/HST NETFILE online service or the Represent a Client online service.
