Call centre service changes: CRA and COVID-19

COVID-19: Contacting the Canada Revenue Agency

  • Call 1-833-966-2099 to speak to an agent about the Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB), Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB), or Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS).
  • Some call centres may have reduced hours, longer wait times, or suspended services.
