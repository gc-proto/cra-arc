Changes to taxes and benefits: CRA and COVID-19

COVID-19: Important updates

  • Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB): You can apply in CRA My Account or over the phone.
  • Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS): Starting April 27, 2020, eligible employers can apply in CRA My Business Account or through a separate online application form.
  • Call 1-833-966-2099 to speak to an agent about CERB or CEWS.
