Become a CVITP volunteer

As a Community Volunteer Income Tax Program (CVITP) volunteer, you’ll participate in free tax clinics that help eligible individuals with their tax returns so they can get the benefit and credit payments they are entitled to. Whether you prepare taxes or assist in a support role, your contribution makes a difference.

  • CVITP applies to residents of:

    • Alberta
    • British Columbia
    • Manitoba
    • New Brunswick
    • Newfoundland and Labrador
    • Nova Scotia
    • Northwest Territories
    • Nunavut
    • Ontario
    • Prince Edward Island
    • Saskatchewan
    • Yukon

  • If you are a Quebec resident, the volunteer program is coordinated by Revenue Québec.

    Refer to: Income Tax Assistance – Volunteer Program
