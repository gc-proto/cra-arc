CRA web service manual

A collection of components, templates, practices, and guidance to support CRA design practitioners in building and maintaining web services.

Services and information

Create and maintain content

Guidance that is inline with web writing practices to create content that is findable, easy to understand, task-oriented, accessible and inclusive.

Design and coding

Detailed gallery of components, functions, templates and features to develop consistent, compliant and accessible web pages.

Research, test, and measure

Guidance on how to verify the reading level of content, and get user experience (UX) research and testing done on your web products.

Optimize and overhaul

Guidance on how to evaluate your existing content, create a user centred design and improve search engine results.

Skills development and training

Basic and advanced skills required to be effective on web related functions, including resources, events, and communities you may want to engage with.

Understand foundational principles

Principles and systems supporting the development of successful web services and products, including information architecture, visual and interaction design, accessibility and inclusion.
