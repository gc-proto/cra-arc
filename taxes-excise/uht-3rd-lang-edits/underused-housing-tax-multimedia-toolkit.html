Underused Housing Tax (UHT) multimedia toolkit

Multimedia promotional material related to the Underused Housing Tax (UHT) can be accessed below in simple and easy-to-follow formats. The UHT is an annual federal 1% tax on the ownership of vacant or underused housing in Canada that took effect on January 1, 2022.

For complete information, refer to: Underused Housing Tax
