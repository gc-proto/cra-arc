About tax rates

The Government of Canada sets the federal income tax rates for individuals.

Each province and territory determines their own income tax rates. Provincial or territorial income tax rates apply in addition to federal income tax rates.

Provincial and territorial tax rates

Provincial and territorial tax rates vary across Canada. However, the tax is calculated in the same way as your federal income tax (except for those who reside in Quebec).

Offseting tax owed with credits, benefits and deductions

There are also various tax credits, deductions and benefits available to you to reduce your total tax payable.
