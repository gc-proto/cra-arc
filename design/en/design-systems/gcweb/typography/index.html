Line length

The line length is contrained to a maximum 65 characters. This ensures that no lines extend beyond a comfortable reading length. This improves the readablity of the content, which improves comprehension.

Page layouts may be wider than 65 characters. The line length restriction applies only to characters in a line of text.

Link appearance

Links in the content area are underlined. The exceptions are:

  • links that look like buttons
  • links in the banner and footer
