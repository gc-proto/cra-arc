Returns and Public Service Bodies' (PSB) rebates can be adjusted electronically using My Business Account. If you do not already have a user ID and password, you can request one at My Business Account.

Adjust a return

This service lets you adjust a previously filed Form GST34, GST/HST Return for Registrants, or Form RC7200, GST/HST and QST Return for Selected Listed Financial Institutions. If you want to adjust another type of return (such as Form GST106, Information on Claims Paid or Credited for Foreign Conventions and Tour Packages), send your request in writing to your tax centre.

Once you have a user ID and password, sign in to My Business Account and select the "Adjust a return" option. You will then be presented with a list of periods that are eligible for electronic adjustment. Select the period you want to adjust. You will then be able to enter your updated information on screens that are very similar to those used in GST/HST NETFILE.

Adjust a PSB rebate

This service lets you electronically ask for an adjustment to an assessed public service bodies' (PSB) rebate claim. This service is available to registered and non-registered public service bodies. If you want to adjust another type of rebate, send your request in writing to the Atlantic Tax Centre.

Once you have a user ID and password, sign into My Business Account and select the "Adjust a PSB rebate" option. You will then be presented with a list of periods that are eligible for electronic adjustment. Select the period you want to adjust. You will then be able to enter your updated information on screens that are very similar to those used in GST/HST NETFILE.
