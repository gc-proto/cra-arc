Due to system maintenance, the RC193 webform will not be available from 3:00AM to 6:00AM daily.

This online form guides you through a series of questions to allow you to provide feedback on your experience that you received from the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). In order for us to improve the services we provide to Canadians, please provide all relevant information while completing this online form.

Please note: This form does not allow you to submit additional supporting documentation. This form may not support older browser versions.

Once you complete this form, we recommend that you print or save a copy of the confirmation for your records.

Service feedback refers to the quality and/or the timeliness of the CRA service. For more information, go to Canada.ca/cra-service-feedback (Note: this link will load in another window or tab) .

Do you know your service rights as a taxpayer?

Taxpayer Bill of Rights
  • 5. You have the right to be treated professionally, courteously, and fairly.
  • 6. You have the right to complete, accurate, clear, and timely information.
  • 9. You have the right to lodge a service complaint and to be provided with an explanation of our findings.
  • 10. You have the right to have the costs of compliance taken into account when administering tax legislation.
  • 11. You have the right to expect us to be accountable.
  • 13. You have the right to expect us to publish our service standards and report annually.
  • 14. You have the right to expect us to warn you about questionable tax schemes in a timely manner.
  • 15. You have the right to be represented by a person of your choice.

Personal information is collected under the Income Tax Act and the Excise Tax Act to administer tax, rebates, and elections. It may also be used for any purpose related to the enforcement of the Act such as audit, compliance and collection activities.
