COVID-19 benefits from the CRA

Status: All benefits are closed

You may be looking for:

  • Wage and rent subsidies

The CRA administered COVID-19 benefits to provide temporary income support to individuals between March 15, 2020 and May 7, 2022. Review each benefit’s eligibility criteria and application periods to validate your application or find out how to repay your benefit overpayments.

Eligibility criteria for closed benefits

Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB) Closed

CERB eligibility was from March 15, 2020 to September 26, 2020, the last day to apply was December 2, 2020

Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) Closed

CRB eligibility was from September 27, 2020 to October 23, 2021, the last day to apply was December 22, 2021

Canada Recovery Caregiving Benefit (CRCB) Closed

CRCB eligibility was from September 27, 2020 to May 7, 2022, the last day to apply was July 6, 2022

Canada Recovery Sickness Benefit (CRSB) Closed

CRSB eligibility was from September 27, 2020 to May 7, 2022, the last day to apply was on July 6, 2022

Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit (CWLB) Closed

CWLB eligibility was from October 24, 2021 to May 7, 2022, the last day to apply was May 18, 2022

Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB) Closed

CESB eligibility was from May 10, 2020 to August 29, 2020, the last day to apply was September 30, 2020
