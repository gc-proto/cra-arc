Unit

A unit is defined as physically connected equipment located in a power plant that operates together to produce electricity by means of thermal energy using coal as a fuel, whether in conjunction with other fuels or not.

This includes boilers and other combustion devices, gasifiers, reactors, turbines, generators, and emission control devices.
