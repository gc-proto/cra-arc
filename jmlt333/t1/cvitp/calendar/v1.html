CVITP training webinars

The Community volunteer income tax program (CVITP) training webinars are sessions hosted by CVITP officers. These training webinars cover a range of topics and are designed to support participants in preparing for the upcoming tax‑filing season.  

All sessions are held on Microsoft Teams. To help get you set up, refer to Microsoft Teams videoconference reference tool for easy installation and guidance.

Recorded sessions

Disclaimer - Recorded sessions

Recordings on this page are provided by CVITP officers from different regions.

All sessions are offered in English. For sessions in French, please refer to Calendrier des webinaires de la formation PCBMI.

Some videos may show older versions of the UFile CVITP software. The content is accurate and generally the only difference is the tax year shown. If a video contains outdated information, it will be removed as soon as possible.
