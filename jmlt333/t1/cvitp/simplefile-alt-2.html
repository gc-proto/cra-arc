Use your SimpleFile invitation to get your taxes done for free

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) offers this service to help lower-income Canadians file their personal tax return.

SimpleFile is a tax filing service offered by the CRA, which allows eligible individuals to file their 2024 income tax and benefit return. The service targets individuals with a lower income or a fixed income, and a simple tax situation that remains unchanged from year to year.

If you are a Quebec resident

The SimpleFile service options only complete the federal tax return. You will still need to complete and submit a Quebec provincial tax return with Revenu Québec.

Ways to SimpleFile

To complete your tax return, the SimpleFile services use the information in CRA's systems at the time of use of the service. Once your return has been assessed, you will receive a notice of assessment (NOA)


SimpleFile by Phone

The invitation letter provides the information you need to use the SimpleFile by Phone service. There are no forms to fill out or calculations to do. You do not need to speak to an agent to use this service.

Eligibility

You do not need to have income to use this service. If you do have income, it must be under a certain threshold and from certain sources.

You must have a recent filing history.

Income under a certain threshold

You must have a lower income or fixed income, based on factors that include age, province of residence, and disability status.

Income from certain sources

You must have income exclusively from the following sources:

Benefits

Old Age Security – T4A(OAS)

Guaranteed Income Supplement / Net federal supplements – T4A(OAS)

Canada or Quebec Pension Plan benefits (including disability benefits) – T4A(P)

Employment and related income supports

Employment income – T4

Employment Insurance Benefits – T4E

Workers’ compensation benefits – T5007

Social assistance payments – T5007

Canada Emergency Benefits – T4A

Investment income

Some Interest Income – T5 (box 13)

Invitations

This service applies to invitations that are sent in both:

  • February 2025
  • July 2025

Your invitation letter will indicate the filing option available to you. If your personal situation has changed, you may no longer be able to complete your return by the filing option indicated in your letter.

How you will receive your invitation
In your online CRA account

Your invitation letter will be available in your CRA My Account. You will be able to access and view your invitation once you are registered.

Delivery by mail

The CRA sends you online mail unless you set your preferences to letter mail, or if you have never provided us with your email address.

Online mail in your CRA account

We will send you email notifications when there is mail in your CRA account.

You will not receive your invitation by letter mail.

Letter mail

We will send your invitation to the mailing address we have on file.

You can also view your invitation in your CRA account, but you will not receive email notifications.

Availability

This service is available every day (only closed between 3 am and 6 am ET), from:

  • February, 19 2025 to January 31, 2026 (for invitations received in February 2025)
  • July 15, 2025 to January 31, 2026 (for invitations received in July 2025)
Completion time

Approximately 5 to 10 minutes

Additional information

To auto-file your tax information by phone, you will need to:

  • Verify some personal information
  • Answer a series of short questions using the keypad on your phone
If you are disconnected from the call

If you hang up or are disconnected from the call before the end of the session, you will have to call again. If you think you have made an error before the end of the session, you can hang up and call back to start over.

Your information is not saved during the session.

If you have a Personal Identification Number (PIN)

At the end of the call, you can receive an estimate of your net income, taxable income, and any refund that you may be eligible for at the end of the call. To receive this information, you will need to provide your PIN when prompted.

For information on how to create a PIN, refer to: Use a PIN when you call us.
