Steps to use SimpleFile Digital

  1. Locate your access code

    SimpleFile Digital requires you have a NETFILE Access Code (NAC). There are 2 ways locate it depending on your situation.

    • If you received an invitation

      On your 2025 SimpleFile invitation

      Where to find the code on your invitation

      If you have received a SimpleFile invitation package, the invitation has the information you need to use the SimpleFile Digital service. This includes the link to the service and your NAC. There are no forms to fill out or calculations for you to do.

      Delivery through CRA account

      If you are registered to receive email notifications, we will send you an email when your SimpleFile invitation package is available to view in your CRA account.

      If you have access to a CRA account, but are not registered for email notifications, you can still view your invitation package in your CRA account.

      Delivery by mail

      We will send your SimpleFile invitation package by mail to the mailing address we have on file, if you are not registered to receive email notifications.

    • If you did not receive an invitation

      On your 2024 tax notice of assessment (NOA)

      Where to find the code on your NOA

      Your unique 8-character NAC is located on the right side of your previous year’s notice of assessment (NOA)

  2. Get your information ready

    Before you use SimpleFile Digital, you will need your:

    • Social insurance number (SIN)
    • Date of birth
    • Mailing address
    • Spouse’s SIN and net income, if married or common-law
    • Access code

  3. File using the SimpleFile Digital web form

    The service is available daily from:

    • February 24, 2025 to January 31, 2026
    • Between 6 am and 3 am (ET)

    • All residents (except Quebec)

      Both the federal tax return and provincial or territory forms are filed.

    • Quebec residents

      Only the federal tax return is filed. You will still need to complete and submit a Quebec provincial income tax return with Revenu Québec.

    SimpleFile Digital web form

  4. Wait for your notice of assessment

    Once your return has been assessed, you will receive a notice of assessment (NOA).

    Keep your supporting documents for 6 years in case the CRA asks to see them.

    If you have already submitted your tax return using SimpleFile and need to make a change, refer to changing a tax return.
