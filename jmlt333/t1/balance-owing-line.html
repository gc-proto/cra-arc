Line 48500 – Balance owing

Subtract the amount on line 48200 of your return from the amount on line 43500 of your return.

If the result is positive, you have a balance owing. Enter this amount on line 48500 of your return.

Pay your balance owing

Do not mail cash or include cash with your return.

There are options to pay the full amount, and options if you cannot pay in full.

The CRA will charge daily compound interest on any outstanding balance starting May 1, 2024, until your balance is paid in full.

Refer to: Paying the balance owing on your personal income tax
