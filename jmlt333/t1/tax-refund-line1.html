Line 48400 - Refund

You may be looking for:

  • Tax refunds
  Amount on line 43500: the total income tax you owe for the year
-  Amount line 48200: the total tax deductions and credits
=  Your result

If the result is negative, you will receive a refund. If you file your tax by paper, enter this amount on line 48400 of your return. Generally, the CRA does not refund a difference of $2 or less. The amount on line 48400 represents your expected refund, however, the refund you receive will be based on the amount shown on your Notice of Assessment (NOA).
