Line 48400 - Refund

Take the amount on line 43500 of your return and substract the amount on line 48200.

For example:

Your result = Amount on line 43500 - Amount on Line 48200

If the result is negative, you have a refund. Enter this amount on line 48400 of your return. This amount represents your expected refund, however, the refund you receive will be based on the amount shown on your Notice of Assessment (NOA). If the NOA shows a different amount, it includes a detailed explanation of any adjustments made by the CRA.

If the result is positive, this is your balance owing. Enter this amount on line 48500.

Refer to: Tax refunds
