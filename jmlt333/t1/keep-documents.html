Personal income tax

Keeping documents

You must keep tax documents for 6 years after filing a personal tax return.

  • If the return was filed online

    Keep your supporting documents even though you don't need to include them when filing.

  • If the return was filed by paper

    Keep your supporting documents even if the return, form, or schedule you are completing indicates you do not have to attach any.

Supporting tax documents can include:

  • Tax information slips
  • Official receipts
  • Bank statements
  • Cancelled cheques

It is important to keep all of your tax documents in case the CRA asks you to provide them later. You should also keep a copy of your tax return and any related notice of assessments.
