Personal income tax

Using a professional tax preparer

You may be looking for:

  • Filing as an electronic service provider

You can take your tax documents to a professional tax preparer, who for a fee, will file your tax return for you.

A tax preparer can be:

  • A firm
  • An organization
  • An individual

Finding a tax preparer

Almost all Canadians are eligible to have their tax return filed electronically. Your tax preparer will confirm based on your situation.

Use our directory to type in the postal code of the area where you would like to find a tax preparer.

A tax preparer can also offer tax discounting services. This is where your refund is calculated, and immediatley part of it is paid to you before your tax return is filed.

For details, refer to: Discounter information

Authorizing your tax preparer

You will authorize your tax preparer to represent you and have access to your tax information.

You will be required to sign a completed Form T183, authorizing the transmission of your return.

Keep all information slips, receipts, and completed Form T183 in case your tax return is selected for a detailed review.
