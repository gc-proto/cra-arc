Personal income tax

How to file a tax return

You may choose to file your own taxes or have someone else file your taxes electronically using tax software or on paper. If eligible, you may receive an invitation or get help to file your taxes for free.

Filing dates for 2024 taxes

  • February 24, 2025 : Earliest day to file your taxes online
  • April 30, 2025 : Deadline to file your taxes
  • June 15, 2025 (June 16, 2025, since June 15 is a Sunday): Deadline to file your taxes if you or your spouse or common-law partner are self-employed

Tax software

Find certified tax software, use CRA services to complete and send a tax return, 2 weeks to process

Using your SimpleFile invitation

For eligible individuals invited to file taxes for free using CRA's filing service

Free tax clinic

Find a volunteer to file your taxes for free if you have a modest income and simple tax situation

Using a professional tax preparer

Find a registered tax professional to file your taxes for a service fee

Paper tax return

Complete a return using the income tax package, send by mail, 8 weeks to process

On behalf of someone who died

Report date of death, access tax records as a representative, file final returns
