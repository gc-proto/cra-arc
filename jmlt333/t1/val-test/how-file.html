Personal income tax

How to file a tax return

You may choose to do your own taxes or have someone else do them for you using tax software or on paper. If eligible, you may be able to get your taxes done for free by invitation or using a free tax clinic.

Filing due dates for 2024 taxes

  • April 30, 2025: Deadline to file your taxes
  • June 15, 2025 (June 16, 2025, since June 15 is a Sunday): Deadline to file your taxes if you or your spouse or common-law partner are self-employed
