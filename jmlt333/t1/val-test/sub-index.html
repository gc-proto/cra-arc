Tax software for filing personal taxes

You may be looking for:

  • Filing as a professional tax preparer

You can use tax software certified by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) to help calculate and file your personal income taxes electronically. An updated list of third party software developers and their certified tax software offerings are provided for each tax year.

Within certified tax software, you can choose how you want to fill in your tax information, send your return to the CRA, and view your notice of assessment (NOA).
