Reduce how much tax your may owe

You may be eligible to claim various deductions, credits, and expenses to reduce the amount of tax you owe.

Example: Basic personal amount

The basic personal amount is a non-refundable tax credit that provides a partial reduction for your income.

It is applied both federally and provincially, or territorially to the first part of your income, within the lowest tax bracket.

If your total taxable income is below the maximum amount, you would not pay any tax.

For more details, refer to basic personal amount.

For all deduction types, refer to claiming deductions, credits, and expenses.
