Changing a personal income tax return

You must wait until you receive your notice of assessment (NOA) before you can request to make changes to your personal income tax return.

Through certified tax software

Determine if you are eligible to change your return using certified tax software.

Processing time: Within 2 weeks

"ReFILE" is an online service that allows individuals and tax preparers send online adjustment requests for personal tax returns using certified tax software.

The ReFILE service is:

  • Open February 19, 2024 until January 24, 2025
  • Available 21 hours a day, from 6am to 3am (Eastern time)
  • Closed for maintenane from 3am to 6am (Eastern time)

You or your tax preparer must have used certified tax software to file the return you are requesting to change.

When changing your own tax return

You must have used certified tax software

When changing someone else's tax return

  • If you are a family member or friend, you must have used certified tax software

  • If you are a professional service provider (such as an accountant or discounter), you must have used EFILE certified tax software

Through CRA My Account

Determine if you are eligible to change your return using your CRA My Account.

Processing time: Within 2 weeks

"Change my return" is a feature in your CRA secure portal that allows you to send online adjustment requests for your personal tax return. It includes questions and messages when the CRA:

  • Needs more information
  • Detects errors
  • Needs supporting documents to finalize your request

You are not able to use "Change my return" to apply for benefits or credits.

By mail

Change your return by mail.

Processing time: Within 8 weeks


Send both of the following to your tax centre:

  • A completed Form T1-ADJ, T1 Adjustment Request that provides the details of your request

  • Supporting documents for the entire amount, including amounts you already claimed but did not previously send documents for

Any request to change a previous return must be sent separately from your current year tax return.

  • If your request for changes is approved

    We will send you a notice of reassessment showing any changes to your return, as well as any refund

  • If your request for changes is denied

    We will send you a letter explaining why we did not make changes you asked for, or why no changes were needed

Complex requests take longer to process

Some requests for changes are more complex and will require additional verification.

Processing time: Typically within 20 weeks

Complex requests include changes related to:

  • Multiple tax returns
  • Tax years beyond the normal 3-year reassessment period
  • A bankruptcy return
  • A deceased taxpayer
  • A carryback amount, such as a capital or non-capital loss
  • The elected split-pension amount
  • A request from the CRA to you or your authorized representative for more information or documents

Requests filed for international and non-resident returns (including emigrant returns) may take longer to process.

For more information on processing times, refer to: CRA processing times
