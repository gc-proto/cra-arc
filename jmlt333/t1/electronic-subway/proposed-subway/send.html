Sending the tax return

Most taxpayers can use "NETFILE" to electronically send personal tax returns, however you must make sure you are eligible to use this service.

Before send the tax return, make sure you know the limitations of the NETFILE service are:

  • Open February 19, 2024 until January 24, 2025
  • Available 21 hours a day, from 6am to 3am (Eastern time)
  • Closed for maintenane from 3am to 6am (Eastern time)

[Refer to: NETFILE Terms and Conditions and Privacy Notice ?]

Eligibility

To use the NETFILE service, you must be one of the following:

  • a Canadian citizen
  • a permanent resident of Canada
  • an individual currently in Canada

You can submit the tax return to the CRA and within a few seconds you will receive a confirmation code.

If you are not eligible to use the NETFILE service, or if you choose to print the tax return and send it, refer to the method of sending a paper return to the CRA.

Confirm the amounts from the return

Use the "Express NOA" online service to immediately verify the amount from the tax return.

[You would have selected this option when choosing your preferences.]

Using the service

Once you file your return and receive your confirmation message, you will be prompted to enter your My Account user ID and password to use the "Express NOA" service.

If you used Auto-fill my return

If you have already entered your user ID and password and used the Auto-fill my return service, you may not need to re-enter them to access the Express NOA service if your login session remains active.

The express NOA will be delivered into your software right after filing your return.

You can save or print it. You will only be able to access your express notice one time.

Tax returns that are filed online are generally assessed so quickly that any payments sent with a return will not show up on an Express NOA. You can confirm that we have received the payment for any amount owing on your tax return.

Proceed with finishing your taxes

There are 3 possible outcomes from your tax return:

  • Get a refund
  • Have a balance owing
  • Have no refund or balance owing

You will receive your notice of assessment (NOA) after your tax return is processed. It will indicate the outcome from your tax return.

If you need to make changes to your tax return, you must wait until you receive your NOA.

Processing time: Within 2 weeks
