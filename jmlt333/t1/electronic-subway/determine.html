Determine which CRA services to use

Text

Auto-fill my return

"Auto-fill my return" is an online service that allows for parts of a personal tax return to be automatically filled in with information the CRA has available at the time.

The service will deliver the:

  • Access code
  • Tax information slips
  • Other tax related information

Ensure you can access our sign-in services

You must be registered for My Account for individuals.

If you are a tax preparer, you must be registered for Represent a Client, and the person you are doing taxes for must be registered for My Account for individuals.

Provincial partner sign-in does not work with this service

The provincial partners Alberta.ca Account or BC Services Card do not work with the "Auto-fill my return" service.

If a provincial partner is your only sign-in credential, you will need to register for a CRA credential or a Sign-in partner.

CRA reponsibilities and validations

The CRA is responsible for making sure personal and financial information is sent in an encrypted format between your computer and our servers. We are also responsible for the security and confidentiality of your information once we receive it, and take precautions to ensure that there is no unauthorized access to your data.

When using this service, the CRA performs several identification validations with the information we have in our database. If it finds an identification error or a discrepancy, the system will automatically stop processing your return.

Know the limitations

If you are filing a Quebec tax return

With the NETFILE service, all provincial and territorial tax returns are filed as schedules to your federal income tax return, except returns for Quebec.

To learn more about filing your Quebec provincial tax return electronically, refer to: Revenu Québec

If you are filing multiple returns

If you are a tax preparer registered in Represent a Client (RAC) to act on behalf of others, you may file up to 20 returns per computer or online account for each tax year.

Tax software companies must respect this limit to be certified for CRA's NETFILE service.

Express NOA

"Express NOA" is an online service that allows the notice of assessment (NOA) or notice of reassessment (NOR) to be viewed in certified tax software, right after the return has been received and processed by the CRA.

How it compares to an NOA or NOR

An express notice is a version of an NOA or NOR. The amount shown through the "Express NOA" service is the final amount that will also show on your NOA or NOR.

  • An express notice

    • Provides basic information
    • Available in tax software immediately after you file
    • Access one time only
    • Does not show your payment

  • An NOA or NOR

    • Provides a complete detailed summary
    • Available in your CRA secure portal or sent by mail after it is processed
    • Access anytime
    • May show your payment if received before it is issued

Ensure you can access our sign-in services

You must be registered for My Account for individuals.

If you are a tax preparer, you must be registered for Represent a Client, and the person you are doing taxes for must be registered for My Account for individuals.
