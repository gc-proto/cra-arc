Excise duty forms

These forms are for use by persons that undertake any excise activity involving approved formulations, denatured alcohol, or specially denatured alcohol; or beer, cannabis, spirits, tobacco, vaping, or wine products.

Legislative sources

  • Excise Act, 2001
  • Excise Act (For beer products only)

On this page

  • Approved formulations, denatured alcohol, and specially denatured alcohol
  • Beer products
  • Cannabis products
  • Spirits products
  • Tobacco products
  • Vaping products
  • Wine products
