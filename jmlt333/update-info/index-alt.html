Update your personal information with the CRA

You must inform the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) of changes to your personal information.

Keeping your information up to date ensures you continue to receive the benefits and payments you are entitled to. It also allows us to contact you if necessary, for example if there are security or identity theft issues with your account.
