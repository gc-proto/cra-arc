Free tax clinics

Community organizations and volunteers help individuals to receive the benefits and credits they are entitled to.

Through the Community Volunteer Income Tax Program (CVITP), participating organizations host free tax clinics where volunteers complete income tax and benefit returns for eligible individuals with a modest income and simple tax situation.

The CVITP program is a partnership between the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) and community organizations and their volunteers. In Quebec, the program is referred to as the Income Tax Assistance – Volunteer Program (ITAVP).
