Pre-claim approval process

Intro.

Requirements for pre-claim approval

To qualify for pre-claim approval, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Your business must be either a Canadian-controlled private corporation or other Canadian corporation, or a partnership
  • You must have a gross business income of less than $25M
  • Your project cannot have already been claimed in a previous tax year
  • Your project cannot involve issues that may be currently under litigation

Steps

Intro text

  1. Complete the request form

    Fill in the initial form to request pre-claim approval and submit to the CRA

  2. Completing your application

    Using your reference number, fill in the form and provide supporting information, submit through your CRA account

  3. Meeting with the CRA

    Contact with your Research and Technology Advisor (RTA), CRA's decision
