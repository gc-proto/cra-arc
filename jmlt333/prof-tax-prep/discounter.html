Using tax discounting services

You may be looking for:

  • Filing as a tax discounting service provider

You can take your tax documents to a professional tax preparer who will file your tax return for a fee.

A professional tax preparer can also offer tax discounting services. This is where your refund is calculated, and immediatley part of it is paid to you before your tax return is filed.

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) conducts compliance programs under the Tax Rebate Discounting Act to make sure:

  • Your rights and interests are protected when using tax discounting services

  • Tax discounters meet their responsibilities under the law when providing tax discounting services
