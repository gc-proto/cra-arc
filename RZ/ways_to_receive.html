Ways to receive requested records

Online

To receive your response package electronically:

  1. Create an ATIP online request service account when you fill out your request
  2. Select “Electronic – to your ATIP online account” as your preferred method to get your records

If you choose to continue as an ATIP online guest, you will have to create a Connect account with Canada Post to receive records for your ATIP request.

The CRA uses this secure online platform to send records that you cannot receive through the ATIP online request service. This service is safe and free.

Create a Connect account

By mail

The requested records will be mailed using Xpresspost™ to the mailing address provided. Xpresspost™ tracks packages and has a 2 day delivery guarantee.

If delivery of the records is unsuccessful, an ATIP analyst may contact you to verify your address.

In person

An ATIP analyst will contact you using the contact information provided. The analyst will arrange a scheduled time for you to view the records at your nearest Tax Service Office or Tax Center.

If delivery of the records is unsuccessful, an ATIP analyst may contact you to verify your address.
