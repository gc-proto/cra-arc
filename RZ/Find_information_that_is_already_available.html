Find information that is available without an ATIP request

You can get many types of information from the CRA without sending an ATIP request.

This is often the fastest way to get the information you need.

The following information that the CRA does not have

  • Information about administering social insurance numbers (applications, replacements for lost or stolen cards, or forgotten numbers) or employment insurance. Go to: Service Canada
  • Information about your travel history outside Canada. Go to: Canada Border Services Agency

Access personal or business information

You can access many types of personal or business information through the CRA’s My Account or My Business Account.

For personal information

Sign in your CRA account and choose My Account, you can find information such as:

  • Personal tax returns, assessments, and statements
  • Information slips (for example, T4, T4A, or T5 slips, and RRSP receipts)
  • Personal information (for example, your address, direct deposit information, and representative authorizations)
Sign in to the CRA's My Account Register for a CRA account

For a complete list of personal information you can get through your CRA account, go to: services in CRA account

You may also call the individual tax enquiries line at 1-800-959-8281 to request tax and benefit documents.

For business information

Sign in your CRA account and choose My Business Account, you can find information such as:

  • Tax returns
  • Corporate account balance
  • Account transactions
  • Notices of assessment or reassessment
Sign in to the CRA's My Business Account Register for a CRA account

For a complete list of information you can get through My Business Account, go to: services in My Business Account

You may also call the business enquiries line at 1-800-959-5525 to request corporate income tax documents.

CRA spending and administrative activities

Many of the records held by the CRA are readily accessible to the public.

You can find information about previously completed access to information requests and about how much CRA spends on certain activities.

  • Travel and hospitality
    • Annual expenditures for travel, hospitality and conferences
    • Hospitality expenditures for senior CRA officials
    • Travel expenditures for senior CRA officials
  • Briefing packages
    • Briefing note titles
    • Question period notes prepared for ministers
  • Contracts, grants, and contributions
    • Contracts over $10,000 issued by or on behalf of the CRA
    • Grants and contributions over $25,000 awarded by the CRA
  • Administrative activities
    • Job reclassifications within the CRA
    • Privacy impact assessment
    • Open data

Find information about registered charities

You do not need to send an ATIP request for information about registered charities and other qualified donees.
