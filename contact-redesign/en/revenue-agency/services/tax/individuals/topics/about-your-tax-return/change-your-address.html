Update your personal information with the CRA

Update your personal information with the CRA – Change your address

If you move, let the CRA know your new address as soon as possible.

Your updated address will allow the CRA to properly validate your identity when you call, and will help to ensure that you receive your payments and correspondence.

Ways to update your address

Choose an option to learn how to make changes to your account.


Online

Processing time: Immediate

Changing your address online using your CRA account is the fastest method. Your address is updated immediately after you submit the change, with no wait time.

CRA sign in

  1. Sign in to your CRA account
  2. Select your Individual account to access My Account
  3. Select Profile
  4. Go to Contact information
  5. Select Edit
  6. Update your address

If you cannot sign in to your CRA account

If your phone number has changed and you cannot receive your multi-factor authentication (MFA) passcode to sign in, you will have to update your information by phone.

For more help with your CRA account, go to: Help with using your CRA account

Make a change for someone else

To change an address online for someone else, including a spouse or family member, you must be their legal representative and use Represent a Client in your CRA account.


By phone

Processing time: Same day

Information you will need before you call

  • Your social insurance number
  • Your full name
  • Your date of birth
  • Your complete address
  • Notice of assessment (for automated services or live agent)
  • An assessed return, notice of reassessment, other tax document, or to be signed in to your CRA account (for live agents only)
Calling on behalf of someone else

You can only call on behalf of someone else in the following 2 situations:

  • The person is present on the call to verify their identity in English or French and can provide verbal authorization for you to speak for them

  • You are the person's legal representative with full legal authority to speak for them

    For details: Authorize a representative

Use an alternative phone number if you live in a Canadian territory, are outside of Canada or the US, or use assistive technology.

By mail

Processing time: 4 to 6 weeks after we receive your documents

Follow these steps to change your address by mail:

  1. Complete Form RC325, Address and Telephone Number Change Request
  2. Mail the completed form to the address on the form

Make a change for someone else

To change the address of another individual, such as your spouse or common-law partner, you must be their legal representative. The form must be completed along with the name of the legal representative clearly mentioned with a signature.


On your tax return

Processing time:

  • Updated at the same time that your return is processed when you file a return electronically
  • Approximately 8 weeks after receiving your paper return
 

You can change your address when you file your tax return in one of the following ways:

  • On your paper return
  • On your return filed electronically through a tax preparer or authorized service provider using EFILE

You cannot change your address when using NETFILE to file your return.

However, you should not wait for tax filing season to update your information. In some cases, identification changes could have a direct impact on your eligibility for benefits.

If you have moved

You may be eligible to claim some of your moving expenses.

Change your address with other government departments

You need to contact each government department that provides services to you to give them your new information.

The CRA will not share your change of address or other personal information with other government departments.
