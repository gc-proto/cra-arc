Claiming the disability tax credit

If you are approved for the disability tax credit (DTC), you may be able to claim the disability amount of $10,138 on line 31600 of your 2025 tax return (in Step 5 of your T1).

Tax tip: If you or anyone else paid for attendant care or for care in a facility, special rules may apply. For more information, read Attendant care and care in a facility

Supplement for persons under 18

If you were under 18 years of age at the end of 2025 and qualify for the disability amount, you may claim up to an additional $5,914.

This supplement may be reduced if, in 2025, one of the following situations applies:

  • Someone claimed child care expenses (line 21400) or attendant care expenses (line 33099 or 33199) for you on their tax return
  • You claimed attendant care expenses (line 21500 or line 33099) on your tax return

Fill out the chart for line 31600 on the Federal Worksheet in your income tax package to calculate your claim.

Doing taxes for someone who died

There may be special considerations when claiming amounts on line 31600 for someone who died.
