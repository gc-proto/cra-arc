Ways to get help with the DTC


Online

You may be able to resolve your question without calling.

CRA account

Use your CRA Account to:

  • View the status of your application
  • Get tax credit information
  • Send new documentation using "Submit documents"
  • CRA sign in
  • Register

Submit documents

Optional

  1. Sign in to your CRA account
  2. In the “Correspondence” section in the left menu, choose “Submit documents”
  3. Select the “Submit documents” button, found after the “Submission history” section
  4. Choose the "Start" button
  5. Follow the instructions within the Submit documents service to attach and send your documents

For additional information: Submit documents online

By phone

Call about the DTC

Information you will need before you call

  • Social insurance number (SIN)
  • Full name and date of birth
  • Complete address
  • Assessed tax return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document, or be signed in to CRA My Account
Calling on behalf of someone else

If you’re calling on behalf of someone else, they must be present on the call to give their consent, or you must be authorized to call on their behalf.

Learn more: Authorize a representative
