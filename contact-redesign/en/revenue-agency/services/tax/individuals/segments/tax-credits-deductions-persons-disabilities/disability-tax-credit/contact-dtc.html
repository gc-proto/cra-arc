Ways to get help with the DTC


Online

You may be able to resolve your question without calling.

CRA account

Use your CRA Account to:

  • View the status of your application
  • Get tax credit information
  • Change previous tax returns once you are approved for the credit
  • Send new documentation using "Submit documents"
  • CRA sign in
  • Register

Change a previous year tax return

  1. Sign in to your CRA account
  2. Select your "Individual" account
  3. Select "Tax returns"
  4. Select "Change my return"
  5. Select the option for DTC
  6. Follow the instructions within the Change my return service to update your tax return

For more information: Claiming for past years

Send documents

  1. Sign in to your CRA account
  2. In the “Correspondence” section in the left menu, choose “Submit documents”
  3. Select the “Submit documents” button, found after the “Submission history” section
  4. Select the "Start" button
  5. Follow the instructions within the Submit documents service to attach and send your documents

For more information: Submit documents online

Chat online with a service agent

You can discuss general account-specific issues with a live CRA agent.

Find the chat widget at the bottom of the CRA account main page (right side) after signing in.

Online chat hours

8 am to 8 pm Eastern time
Monday to Friday

Except public holidays


By phone

Call about the DTC

Information you will need before you call

  • Your social insurance number
  • Your full name
  • Your date of birth
  • Your complete address
  • Notice of assessment (for automated services or live agent)
  • An assessed return, notice of reassessment, other tax document, or to be signed in to your CRA account (for live agents only)
Calling on behalf of someone else

If you’re calling on behalf of someone else, they must be present on the call to give their consent, or you must be authorized to call on their behalf.

Learn more: Authorize a representative

Phone number

All of Canada and United States except Yukon, Northwest Territories and Nunavut, see below for specified numbers 1-800-959-8281 (opens up phone application)
To speak with a live service agent

Use an alternative phone number if you live in a Canadian territory, are outside of Canada or the US, or use assistive technology.

Alternative phone numbers

Specific phone numbers for certain situations:

  • Yukon, Northwest Territories and Nunavut residents

    1-866-426-1527 (opens up phone application)

    Only use this number if your area code is 867. If your area code is something else, call the general number at 1-800-959-8281.

    Service hours are the same as those mentioned above for live service agents. You will be connected almost immediately.

  • Located outside of Canada and the U.S.

    1-613-940-8495 (opens up phone application)

    The CRA only accepts collect calls made through a phone operator. Once your call is connected, you might hear a beep and experience a short delay.

    Wait times and service hours are the same as those mentioned above for live service agents.

  • TTY (Teletypewriter) and Canada VRS (Video Relay Service)
    TTY number
    1-800-665-0354
    Canada VRS registration

    Download the SRV Canada app to use the VRS phone line.

    For more information, refer to Canada VRS.

    Service hours are the same as those mentioned above for live service agents. You will be connected almost immediately.
