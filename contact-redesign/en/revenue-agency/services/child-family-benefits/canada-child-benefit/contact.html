Get support for an issue

If your personal information or custody arrangements have changed, you can complete most of your updates online in your CRA account.

This is often the easiest and fastest way to provide changes to us.

Help with common topics

  • Your payments have stopped or changed

    You must file your tax return every year and meet other conditions to continue receiving your CCB and related provincial and territorial payments.

    For more information: Why your payments may have stopped or changed

Ways to get help with CCB


Online

You may be able to resolve your question without calling.

CRA account

Use your CRA Account to:

  • Apply for the Canada child benefit (CCB)
  • View payments, account balance, and statement of account
  • Update your personal information, including your address and your marital status
  • View and update information about children in your care
  • Chat with a service agent
  • CRA sign in
  • Register

Chat online with a service agent

You can discuss general account-specific issues with a live CRA agent.

Locate the chat widget at the bottom of the CRA account main page (right side) after signing in.

Paying back CCB amounts

Paying back CCB amounts

If you received too much CCB or other child and family benefits, you need to repay them.

Call us to make a payment arrangment if you need to repay the amount over time.

Information you will need before you call

  • Social Insurance Number
  • Personal details about the children in your care
  • Information from previous benefit payments
  • Details from your account or an assessed return, notice of assessment, reassessment, or other tax document
