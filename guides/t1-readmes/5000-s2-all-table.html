Download instructions for fillable PDFs

To complete a fillable form, you must download the accessible fillable PDF to your computer. Use Acrobat Reader 10 or later to open the file. Do not click the link to open it in your web browser.

  1. Download and save the PDF to your computer
  2. Open the downloaded PDF in Acrobat Reader 10 or later
