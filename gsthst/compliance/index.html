GST/HST compliance and enforcement

You may be looking for: CRA compliance and enforcement

The CRA conducts reviews to make sure GST/HST registrants are meeting their tax obligations. The CRA contacts GST/HST registrants either by letter, by phone or by sending out a notification to advise that their account has been selected for review.

GST/HST compliance programs

Learn more about the reasons for a review, how to prepare, records to keep and common errors depending on the GST/HST compliance program

With BCES GST/HST trust account examinations

Examinations are done to make sure you have met all your GST/HST reporting obligations

With PLS (to be done) GST/HST delinquent filer reviews

Reviews are done to obtain outstanding GST/HST returns from non-compliant registrants, and to encourage future voluntary compliance
