Find out if you have to remit (pay) GST/HST by instalments

You need to determine if you need to pay the GST/HST by instalments in your second fiscal year because special rules may apply to you if you have registered for GST/HST in the current fiscal year.

If you are an annual filer and your net tax for your previous fiscal year is $3,000 or more, you may have to make quarterly instalment payments in the current fiscal year.

  What happens if your estimation is wrong

If your actual next fiscal year net GST/HST amount is $3,000 or more, you would be required to pay instalments. The CRA charges instalment interest on instalment payments paid late or that you did not pay.

For example, if your actual next fiscal year net GST/HST amount is (), the CRA charges instalment interest on the required instalment payments.

Learn more on the methods to pay: Pay the GST/HST by instalments

  What happens if your estimation is wrong

If you realize at any time during the fiscal year that you paid less than your required instalment payment or that you did not pay an instalment on time, you can reduce or eliminate your instalment interest by overpaying your next instalment payment or by paying it early.
